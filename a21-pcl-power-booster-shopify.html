‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What makes PCL Power Booster different from traditional HA fillers?

A. PCL Power Booster is a biostimulator that works by stimulating your body's natural collagen production, rather than simply adding volume. It provides long-lasting regenerative benefits (up to 1 year or more) and improves overall skin quality, while HA fillers provide immediate volume but typically last 6-12 months.

Q. How many treatments will I need to see results?

A. The recommended primary course consists of 3 sessions performed once a month for three consecutive months. Results are cumulative and progressive, with optimal outcomes visible after completing the full course. Maintenance treatments are suggested every 3-6 months thereafter.

Q. Is the treatment painful?

A. The liquid formula with nano-sized particles allows for smooth injection using fine needles (30G-31G), which minimizes discomfort. While this formulation does not contain Lidocaine, your practitioner may apply topical anesthetic if needed.

Q. Can I combine PCL Power Booster with other treatments?

A. Yes, PCL Power Booster can generally be used in combination with non-crosslinked skin enhancers or Botulinum Toxin. However, recommended interval periods should be observed, and combination treatments should only be performed under professional guidance.

Q. What should I expect after treatment?

A. Mild swelling, redness, and/or bruising may occur at the injection site but usually resolve within one week. Results develop gradually as collagen production is stimulated, with progressive improvement over the following weeks and months.

Q. Can PCL Power Booster be dissolved if I'm not satisfied?

A. No, PCL is NOT dissolvable with hyaluronidase like HA fillers. This is why proper injection technique and placement by an experienced professional is essential. Discuss your expectations thoroughly with your practitioner before treatment.

Q. Is PCL Power Booster suitable for people with HA allergies?

A. Yes, PCL Power Booster is often well-tolerated and can be used as an alternative for patients with sensitivities or allergies to Hyaluronic Acid products. However, always consult with your healthcare provider about your specific allergies.

Q. Which areas can be treated with PCL Power Booster?

A. PCL Power Booster is versatile and can be used on the full face, neck, d√©collet√©, back of hands, and periorbital area. It's particularly effective for treating wrinkles and skin laxity across larger treatment areas.
