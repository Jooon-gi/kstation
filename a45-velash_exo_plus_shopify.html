Key Ingredients & Mechanism

CICA Exosome (Centella Asiatica Callus Extracellular Vesicles)

High-purity, plant-derived exosomes containing approximately 500 million nanovesicles per ml. These act as tiny nanocarriers facilitating cell-to-cell communication, delivering essential signaling molecules to damaged scalp and hair follicle cells. This process stimulates cell renewal, reduces inflammation, and rejuvenates hair follicles, promoting a healthy environment for hair growth.

PDRN (Sodium DNA/HPDRN 10,000 ppm)

Derived from salmon DNA, PDRN works to increase scalp hydration, support the reconstruction of damaged scalp tissue, and improve microcirculation (blood flow), which is vital for nourishing hair follicles. It also provides an antioxidant barrier against environmental stress.

Growth Factor Complex & Peptides

Includes Copper Tripeptide-1, Biotinoyl Tripeptide-1, Nicotinoyl Tripeptide-1, Galloyl Pentapeptide-74 (SEATIDE), Myristoyl Pentapeptide-17, along with 8 amino peptides and 5 natural enzymes. These molecules directly nourish hair roots, strengthen follicle activity, and support the production of collagen and elastin in the scalp, necessary for maintaining hair bulb integrity and density.

Additional Components

Atelocollagen, Biotin, and botanical extracts including Ecklonia Cava Extract and Hydrolyzed Pea Protein enhance the formula's regenerative properties and provide comprehensive scalp nourishment.

Usage Protocol

Important: This product is designed for professional topical/cosmetic application only and is not intended for injectable use.
1
Preparation
Apply the solution to clean, slightly moist scalp. Ensure the treatment area is free from other topical products.
2
Application Method
For simple topical use, apply and gently massage for 1-3 minutes until fully absorbed. For optimal professional results, a second application followed by use with an AMTS/Microneedling device (0.25mm to 0.5mm depth) is recommended to maximize penetration.
3
Treatment Frequency
Apply once per week for a full course (4 to 7 weeks), followed by maintenance treatments as needed. Visible results may appear as early as 30 days of consistent use.
4
Post-Treatment Care
Avoid washing the treated area for 24 hours. Minimize exposure to heat or harsh chemical treatments. Consider complementary LED Light Therapy to enhance regeneration and reduce redness.
