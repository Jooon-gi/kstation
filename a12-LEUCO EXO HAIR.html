Mechanism of Action
The specialized exosomes and growth factors are delivered directly to hair follicles, where they stimulate dormant follicle cells, prolong the anagen (growth) phase of the hair cycle, and reduce scalp inflammation—a common contributor to hair loss. This multi-targeted approach supports scalp regeneration and creates an optimal environment for robust hair growth.
Cellular Communication
High-potency signaling molecules are delivered directly to dermal papilla cells, which are essential for robust hair cycle progression. This enhances cellular survival and communication within the hair matrix, addressing root causes of non-scarring hair loss.

Treatment Protocol & Application

Professional administration ensures optimal delivery and absorption of active ingredients.

Professional Administration
Treatment must be administered by trained professionals using micro-needling (MTS), derma roller, or specialized scalp injection (mesotherapy) techniques.
Deep Follicle Penetration
Application methods ensure active ingredients reach deep into hair follicles for maximum efficacy.
Intensive Treatment Course
One session every 1-2 weeks for a total of 4-8 sessions during the initial intensive phase.
Maintenance Therapy
Following the intensive course, maintenance sessions are recommended once a month or every few months based on individual response.
