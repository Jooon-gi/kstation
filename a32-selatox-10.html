Usage Protocol & Treatment Guide

Recommended Treatment Cycle 3-5 sessions at 1-2 week intervals for optimal results

Treatment Areas

  • Face: Entire facial area including forehead, around eyes, nasolabial folds
  • Neck: Front and sides of the neck
  • Décolleté: Chest area for comprehensive rejuvenation
  • Hands: Back of hands for anti-aging treatment

Application Methods

Professional Injection (Mesotherapy)

  • Dosage: 0.1ml per injection point
  • Spacing: Approximately 1cm between points
  • Technique: Intradermal or subdermal injection
  • Note: Professional use only by trained specialists

Microneedling Application (MTS)

  • Devices: Hydra Stamp, Hydra Roller, Dr. Pen, or nano-needling machines
  • Method: Apply thin layer to clean skin, then use device until absorbed
  • Preparation: Ensure treatment area is thoroughly cleaned and disinfected

Treatment Protocol Notes

  • Shake the vial well before use
  • Use one 5ml vial per treatment session
  • Ensure proper sterile technique throughout application
  • Professional application by trained cosmetologists, aestheticians, or doctors recommended
