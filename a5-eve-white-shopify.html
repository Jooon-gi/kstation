5 Key Active Ingredients
Glutathione
Master antioxidant that provides powerful skin brightening effects by reducing melanin production and protecting against environmental damage. Known as the ultimate whitening agent for achieving luminous, even-toned complexion.
PDRN (Polydeoxyribonucleotide / Sodium DNA)
Derived from salmon DNA, this advanced ingredient promotes cellular regeneration, tissue repair, and wound healing. Significantly enhances skin elasticity and accelerates the natural renewal process for healthier, more resilient skin.
Exosome (Plant-Derived)
Often sourced from Centella Asiatica, these microscopic vesicles regulate cell activity and promote deep skin regeneration. Provides powerful anti-inflammatory benefits while stimulating cellular renewal at the deepest levels.
Acetyl Hexapeptide-8
A potent peptide that acts as a "botox-like" agent, helping to improve skin elasticity and dramatically reduce the appearance of wrinkles and fine lines. Works to maintain youthful firmness and smooth texture.
Sodium Hyaluronate (Hyaluronic Acid)
A highly effective form of Hyaluronic Acid that deeply attracts and binds moisture to the skin. Provides intense hydration and a visible plumping effect, resulting in smoother, more supple skin texture.
