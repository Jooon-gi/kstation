Usage Protocol & Treatment Guide

Treatment Areas

Under-eye bags, double chin (submental fat), nasolabial folds, cheekbones, and nasal tissue.
1
Area Assessment
Evaluate treatment area and determine appropriate injection points. For under-eye bags, use 5 specific injection points spaced 0.3-0.5mm apart.
2
Preparation
Cleanse and sterilize the treatment area. Ensure proper technique and sterile conditions are maintained throughout the procedure.
3
Injection Technique
Inject directly into the subcutaneous fat layer (2-6mm depth) using a syringe. Standard protocol uses 1ml per side for under-eye treatment.
4
Volume Guidelines
Total volume per session must not exceed 5ml. Adjust dosage based on treatment area: 1.0-2.0ml for under-eye bags, 2.0-5.0ml for double chin/face.
5
Treatment Cycle
Typically 4-6 sessions recommended, performed at 10-14 day intervals to allow proper fat breakdown and lymphatic clearance.

Storage & Handling

Store the product in its original packaging in a cool, dry place (2°C - 25°C) away from direct sunlight and excessive heat. Do not freeze. Keep out of reach of children. Use within expiration date printed on packaging.

Safety Information & Precautions

Important Safety Information
This product is intended strictly for professional use by trained medical personnel. Proper injection technique and sterile conditions must be maintained at all times.
  • Must be administered by certified medical professionals only
  • Adhere strictly to recommended dosage and injection depth guidelines
  • Maintain 10-14 day intervals between treatment sessions
  • Do not exceed 5ml total volume per session

Treatment Interval Precautions

Adhere strictly to the recommended 10-14 day interval between sessions to allow the body sufficient time for fat breakdown and lymphatic clearance. This interval is critical for optimal results and safety.

Post-Treatment Recommendations

  • Avoid makeup application for 12 hours after treatment
  • Refrain from strenuous exercise for up to one week
  • Avoid alcohol consumption, saunas, and excessive sun exposure for one week
  • Apply ice pack or medical repair mask to soothe skin immediately after procedure

Contraindications

Do not use on patients who are pregnant, breastfeeding, have active skin infections, bleeding disorders, or known allergies to any of the ingredients. Consultation and medical history assessment are required before treatment.
