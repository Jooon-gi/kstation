Treatment Protocol

Recommended Treatment Course

  • Number of Sessions: 2 to 3 treatment sessions for optimal results
  • Interval Between Sessions: 2 weeks apart
  • Evaluation: Additional treatment may be determined by a professional after the third session
  • Volume Per Session: 2ml applied into the dermis layer

Treatment Areas

  • Entire face for general rejuvenation
  • Areas with wrinkles and fine lines
  • Skin sagginess zones (e.g., cheeks, jawline)
  • Areas with scars and post-acne spots

Application Methods

Applied into the dermis layer using professional techniques:
  • MTS Roller: Microneedling technique
  • Meso-stamp: Controlled delivery method
  • Mesotherapy: General injection techniques

Proper depth and technique are crucial for optimal PCL delivery and collagen induction.
