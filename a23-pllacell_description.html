Protocol (Treatment Instructions)

Product Preparation

Supplied as a dry powder, the product must be reconstituted with 4 to 6cc of sterile water for injection before use. Shake thoroughly and allow it to stand for approximately 3 minutes.

Application Depth by Treatment Area

Superficial to Mid-Dermis (Generally 1.0mm to 2.0mm depth). The application is more superficial than traditional PLLA fillers.

Recommended Delivery Method

Mesotherapy technique (microinjections), Multi-needle injector, Microneedling, or BAP (Bio Aesthetic Point) technique.

Treatment Cycle

A course of 3 to 4 sessions is typically recommended, spaced 3 to 4 weeks apart, depending on skin condition and desired results. Results develop gradually over 4 to 6 weeks.

Key Ingredients and Technical Features

  • Biostimulation Mechanism: Combines the structural restoration of PLLA with the cellular repair of PN, creating a synergistic effect for holistic skin rejuvenation.
  • Formulation Type: Lyophilized, sterile powder (must be reconstituted). Supplied in glass vials.
  • Microparticle Size: PLLA microparticles are designed to be smooth for superficial injection without causing lumps, unlike deep-injection PLLA fillers.
  • Gel Structure: Upon reconstitution, it forms a liquid solution/suspension suitable for superficial dermal injection, not a high-viscosity cross-linked gel like traditional HA fillers.

Which Product is Right for Me?

PLLACELL is ideal if your goal is overall skin quality improvement, including elasticity, tone, texture, and hydration, rather than immediate, structural volume replacement.

It is a non-cross-linked product designed to trigger the skin's natural regeneration process for a long-lasting, natural glow.
