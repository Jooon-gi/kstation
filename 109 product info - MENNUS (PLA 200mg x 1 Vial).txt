## 0. Product Name: MENNUS (PLA 200mg x 1 Vial)
- The product is a single vial containing 200mg of lyophilized powder.
- Manufacturer: Maypharm Co., Ltd.

## 1. Key Ingredients & Concentration
- This product contains two main ingredients: Poly-D,L-Lactic Acid (PLA) and Carboxymethyl cellulose (CMC).
- The concentration is a total of 200mg of these mixed ingredients per vial.
- The formulation is a biostimulator that promotes neocollagenesis. While not a hyaluronic acid filler, it is designed to restore and maintain facial volume by stimulating the body's natural collagen production over an extended period.
- The product does not contain Lidocaine, but it can be reconstituted with 1-2 mL of 2% Lidocaine for improved patient comfort during treatment.

## 2. Efficacy Benefits
- Long-lasting collagen regeneration and synthesis.
- Restoration of lost facial volume and contours.
- Improvement of fine and deep wrinkles.
- Enhancement of skin elasticity, firmness, and overall texture.
- Provides an immediate volume effect from the CMC gel, followed by a sustained effect as the PLA particles stimulate new collagen growth.

## 3. Protocol (Instructions)
- Injection Layer by Treatment Area: Dermal layer or subcutaneous layer.
- Recommended Needle Gauge: An 18G needle is used to withdraw the solution, and a 26G needle is used for injection into the skin.
- Treatment Cycle: 1 to 3 treatments are recommended. A single treatment session can provide immediate volume for 3-9 months, with secondary efficacy from collagen regeneration lasting 6 to 24 months.

## 4. Key Ingredients and Technical Features
- Cross-linking Method: Not a cross-linked gel. It is a freeze-dried powder consisting of spherical PLA particles and CMC gel.
- Gel Structure: Powder for reconstitution. The spherical particle shape of the PLA ensures even volume creation and prevents needle blockage.
- Formulation Type: 1 vial containing 200mg of product.

## 5. Version or Treatment Strategy Comparison Table
This product is a single formulation and does not have versions with different physical properties like viscosity or particle size. It is generally applied to various facial areas for the purpose of volume restoration and collagen stimulation.

## Which product is right for me?
MENNUS (PLA 200mg x 1 Vial) is suitable for individuals seeking a long-term solution for improving wrinkles and restoring facial volume by stimulating the body's natural collagen production.
