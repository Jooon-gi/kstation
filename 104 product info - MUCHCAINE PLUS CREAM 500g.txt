## 0. Product Name: MUCHCAINE PLUS CREAM 500g

## 1. Key Ingredients and Formulation
- Ingredients: Lidocaine 10.56%, Benzalkonium Chloride 0.2mg (as a preservative)
- Formulation: White cream, 500g container (jar/tub)
- Application Method: Topical application to intact skin before a procedure. For enhanced absorption, an occlusive dressing or wrap may be used.

## 2. Summary of Action Characteristics
- Onset of Action: Approximately 15-30 minutes after application. Onset time may vary depending on skin type, thickness, and sensitivity.
- Duration of Action: Up to 1-2 hours, with some procedures like tattooing or hair removal potentially extending the effect up to 3-4 hours.
- Main Application Areas: Anesthesia for various cosmetic and dermatological treatments including face, neck, hands, and other body areas.

## 3. Usage Protocol (Application Guide)
- When to Use: Apply to the treatment area 15-30 minutes before the procedure.
- Dosage: A maximum recommended dose of 0.5g is suggested for small areas. Apply a generous layer to the treatment area.
- Application Method: Thoroughly cleanse and dry the area. Apply a thick layer of cream and, for increased effectiveness, cover with a plastic wrap or occlusive dressing.
- When to Remove: Remove the dressing and gently wipe off any excess cream just before the procedure.

| Area | Recommended Dosage | Recommended Application Time | Precautions |

| Tattooing & Permanent Cosmetics | Apply a thick layer | 30-60 minutes | Do not apply to open wounds or inflamed skin. |

## When is it good to use?
This anesthetic cream is ideal for professionals seeking a topical solution to reduce pain before cosmetic and dermatological procedures such as needle injections, dermal fillers, laser treatments, MTS, permanent cosmetics, and hair removal.
