Therapeutic Benefits & Effects

Cosmetic Applications

Temporary improvement of moderate to severe glabellar lines (frown lines) related to corrugator and/or procerus muscle activity. Also effective for treating forehead wrinkles, crow's feet, and wrinkles near the mouth.

Wrinkle Reduction Facial Rejuvenation Non-Surgical Treatment

Medical Applications

Effective treatment for benign essential blepharospasm and upper limb local muscle spasticity associated with stroke. Provides significant therapeutic benefits for muscle-related conditions.

  • Relief of involuntary eyelid spasms (blepharospasm)
  • Treatment of post-stroke upper limb spasticity
  • Muscle relaxation for therapeutic purposes
  • Minimally invasive alternative to surgical intervention

Duration and Onset

Initial effects typically visible within 3 days post-injection, with peak results achieved in 1-2 weeks. Treatment effects generally last 3-4 months, with some cases lasting up to 4-6 months.

Usage Protocols & Administration

1
Preparation & Reconstitution
Reconstitute with 2.5 mL of 0.9% preservative-free sterile saline. This results in a concentration of 4 units/0.1 mL. Gently swirl to dissolve powder without shaking.
2
Dosage Calculation
For glabellar lines: 20 units total divided into 5 injection sites (4 units per site). For blepharospasm: 1.25-2.5 units (0.05-0.1 mL) per site initially.
3
Administration Technique
Perform intramuscular injections using sterile technique. Target corrugator and procerus muscles for glabellar lines with precise placement for optimal results.
4
Post-Treatment Care
Monitor for initial effects within 3 days. Full results typically achieved in 1-2 weeks. Schedule follow-up and plan next treatment session after 3-4 months.
