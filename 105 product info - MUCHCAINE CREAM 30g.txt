## 0. Product Name: Muchcaine Cream 30g
- Muchcaine Cream 30g
- 1 x 30g tube

## 1. Key Ingredients and Formulation
- Ingredients: Lidocaine 10.56%
- Formulation: White to yellowish cream / Tube type / 30g
- Application Method: Topical application on the skin

## 2. Summary of Action Characteristics
- Onset of Action: Approx. 15-30 minutes after application
- Duration of Action: May vary, but generally 1-2 hours
- Main Application Areas: Local anesthesia before injection procedures, pain relief in sensitive skin areas.

## 3. Usage Protocol (Application Guide)
- When to Use: Apply 15 to 30 minutes before a procedure for most procedures. Longer application times (up to 1 hour) may be needed for some procedures like hair removal or tattoos.
- Dosage: A maximum recommended dose for adults is 0.5g. Apply a thick layer to the desired area, but do not use in large quantities, particularly over raw or blistered surfaces.
- Application Method: Spread a thick layer of cream over the skin. A dressing or occlusive film (like plastic wrap) can be used to cover the cream and promote absorption, but some sources state a dressing is not required.
- When to Remove: Wipe off the cream gently just before the procedure.

## When is it good to use?
This is a topical anesthetic cream formulated to provide a strong numbing effect, making it suitable for use before cosmetic procedures, injections, and other minor treatments where pain reduction is desired.
