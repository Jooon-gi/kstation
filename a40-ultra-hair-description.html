How Exosomes Work

Exosomes are nano-sized extracellular vesicles released by stem cells. When administered to the scalp, they act as biological messengers, delivering their potent cargo (growth factors like VEGF, FGF, HGF, and signal proteins) directly to the dermal papilla cells and hair follicle stem cells.

Key Actions
  • Activate Dormant Follicles: Promote the transition of hair follicles from the resting (Telogen) phase back to the active growth (Anagen) phase
  • Prolong the Anagen Phase: Extend the lifespan of growing hair
  • Reduce Hair Follicle Apoptosis: Inhibit programmed cell death in hair cells
  • Modulate Scalp Environment: Reduce inflammation and promote angiogenesis (new blood vessel formation) for enhanced nutrient supply

Main Expected Effects

  • Significant reduction in pathological hair shedding and stabilization of hair loss progression (Androgenetic Alopecia, Telogen Effluvium)
  • Increase in hair density and overall hair count due to activation of dormant follicles
  • Improved hair quality and thickness (increased hair shaft diameter)
  • Scalp health rejuvenation by reducing inflammation and creating an optimal microenvironment for hair growth

Detailed Product Information

High-purity Stem Cell Exosomes. The accompanying diluent usually contains stabilizing agents, moisture-binding ingredients like Hyaluronic Acid, and possibly complementary growth-stimulating peptides.

Delivers thousands of active molecules that communicate with hair follicle cells, leading to robust hair growth signaling, prolonged hair life, and significant revitalization of the scalp matrix. This process is highly sophisticated and minimally invasive.

How to Use

The lyophilized powder must be reconstituted with the provided solution just prior to the procedure to ensure the highest activity of the exosomes. It is then delivered to the scalp typically at a depth suitable for reaching the dermal papilla.

Suitable For

Both men and women with active hair loss who are seeking a highly advanced, non-surgical regenerative solution. It is particularly effective for those sensitive to or looking for alternatives to systemic drug therapies.

As a high-technology regenerative aesthetic product, the purity and concentration of the stem cell exosomes are key to its clinical efficacy. The treatment is well-tolerated, with minimal downtime compared to surgical options.

Precautions and Storage

Storage Temperature

The Lyophilized Powder component must be stored refrigerated (2°C - 8°C) or as specifically directed by the manufacturer, typically in a dark environment. The Activating Solution may be stored at room temperature, but it is best to follow the strict temperature guidelines for the main exosome component.

Time Limit for Use After Opening

Once the powder is reconstituted (mixed) with the diluent, the solution must be used immediately (within 1 hour) and any remaining product must be safely discarded to prevent degradation and contamination.

General Precautions
  • Strictly for professional, topical application or sterile injection only
  • Avoid exposure to extreme heat
  • Ensure the scalp is clean and free of active infection or significant irritation prior to application
  • Avoid aggressive shampooing or excessive sweating for 24-48 hours post-procedure
