- Ingredients: Lidocaine 10.56%
- Formulation: White to yellowish cream / Jar type / 500g

## 2. Summary of Action Characteristics
- Onset of Action: Approximately 15 minutes after application, but may vary with skin type and sensitivity.
- Duration of Action: Varies by procedure, with effects lasting from 15 minutes up to 1 hour or more.
- Main Application Areas: Suitable for various areas requiring topical anesthesia before cosmetic procedures.

## 3. Usage Protocol (Application Guide)
- When to Use: Apply to the desired area before the planned procedure.
- Dosage: Apply a thick layer of cream to the treatment area. The maximum recommended dose for adults is 0.5g.
- Application Method: Spread a generous amount of cream on the skin. A dressing or wrap may be used to enhance absorption.
- When to Remove: Wipe off the cream just before the procedure begins.
