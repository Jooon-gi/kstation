Usage

The 300-unit volume is best suited for large-scale applications. It provides the necessary units for comprehensive body contouring treatments, such as in the calves or trapezius, and for managing conditions like severe hyperhidrosis. It is also an economical choice for practitioners performing multiple procedures.

Features

The product guarantees high patient satisfaction due to its precise and uniform effect. The potency is tightly controlled to ensure consistent results. Its large and homogeneous toxin complex minimizes diffusion beyond the injection site, allowing for localized action.

This version, like others in the BOTULAX series, has a rapid onset of action, with patients typically noticing effects shortly after the procedure.

üß™ Usage Guide & Application Protocols

1
Reconstitution
Reconstitution is typically performed using 0.9% sterile saline solution. The amount of diluent determines the concentration of the final solution. Diluting with 3.0mL of saline results in a concentration of 10.0 units per 0.1mL. Always follow a clinical guide for specific reconstitution volumes.
2
Dosing & Unit Distribution
For cosmetic facial procedures, clinical standards often suggest 20 units for glabellar lines. However, the 300U volume is more suitable for treating larger areas. The large volume allows for comprehensive treatment of multiple areas or large muscle groups in a single session.
3
Onset & Duration
Initial effects can be observed within 2-3 days, with the full cosmetic effect becoming visible within 1-2 weeks. The average duration of effect is approximately 4-6 months, depending on the individual, dosage, and treatment area.
4
Post-Treatment Care
Monitor for initial effects and provide appropriate aftercare instructions. The product should be used immediately after reconstitution, and any unused portion must be discarded.
