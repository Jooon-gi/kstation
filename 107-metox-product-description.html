Usage Protocol & Administration

1
Preparation & Reconstitution
Reconstitute vacuum-dried powder with sterile, preservative-free 0.9% normal saline using 2.5ml for 100-unit vial to achieve 4 units/0.1ml concentration.
2
Gentle Mixing
Gently swirl the vial to mix the solution. Do not shake vigorously to prevent protein denaturation.
3
Treatment Area Assessment
Qualified medical professional evaluates treatment area and determines appropriate dosage and injection points.
4
Precise Administration
Administer according to recommended units per treatment area: Glabellar lines (20-30 units), Forehead lines (10-20 units), Crow's feet (10-15 units per side).
5
Post-Treatment Care
Monitor patient response and schedule follow-up. Minimum interval between treatments should be 3 months.

Recommended Dosage Guidelines

Dosage and injection points must be determined by certified medical professionals based on individual patient assessment and treatment objectives.

Safety Precautions & Warnings

Important Safety Information

Professional medical supervision required. Not suitable for all patients.
  • Do not use on pregnant or breastfeeding individuals
  • Avoid use in patients with known hypersensitivity to ingredients
  • Minimum 3-month interval between treatments required
  • Discard any unused portion after 24 hours of reconstitution

Contraindications

Not recommended for patients with neuromuscular disorders, active skin infections at injection sites, or known allergies to botulinum toxin products.

Professional Administration Only

Must be administered by qualified healthcare professionals with proper training in botulinum toxin injection techniques and facial anatomy knowledge.
