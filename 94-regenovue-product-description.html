Safety Information & Storage

Important Precautions — Must be administered by qualified medical professionals. Patients should disclose any allergies, especially to hyaluronic acid or lidocaine.

Storage Requirements

  • Store at 2-25°C away from direct sunlight
  • Single-use product - discard any remaining after opening
  • Do not freeze or expose to extreme temperatures
  • Check expiration date before use

Contraindications

  • Known hypersensitivity to hyaluronic acid
  • Pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Active skin infections at treatment site
  • History of severe allergic reactions
