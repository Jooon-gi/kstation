FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
WHAT MAKES SEDYFILL DIFFERENT FROM OTHER BODY FILLERS?
Sedyfill utilizes unique HENM cross-linking technology creating a highly viscous and cohesive gel structure that maintains shape without spreading to adjacent tissues, ensuring precise contouring and longer-lasting results up to 2 years.
WHAT IS THE EXPECTED TIMELINE FOR VISIBLE RESULTS?
Results are immediately visible after treatment with continued improvement over the following weeks. Full effects develop within 2-4 weeks and can last 12-15 months, with potential extension up to 2 years with proper care.
WHAT AREAS CAN BE TREATED WITH SEDYFILL BODY FILLER?
Sedyfill is designed for non-surgical breast enhancement, buttocks augmentation, leg asymmetry correction, intimate contouring, and smoothing skin surface irregularities after liposuction procedures.
IS SEDYFILL SAFE AND BIOCOMPATIBLE?
Yes, Sedyfill is made from 100% non-animal derived hyaluronic acid, making it safe and biologically compatible with the human body. However, professional medical administration is required.
CAN TREATMENTS BE REPEATED FOR MAINTENANCE?
Yes, repeat treatments can be performed to maintain desired effects. The treatment cycle allows for reapplication as needed to sustain optimal body contouring results.
WHAT INJECTION TECHNIQUE IS RECOMMENDED?
Injection should be performed at the subcutaneous layer or deep dermis level. Use of a cannula is often recommended for large volume body filler applications to ensure optimal distribution and patient comfort.
