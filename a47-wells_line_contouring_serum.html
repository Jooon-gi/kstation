Facial Contouring Strategy

Target Areas: Double chin, defined jawline contour (jowls), chubby cheeks

Application Method: Local injection into subcutaneous fat layer using fine gauge syringe. Inject small amounts (0.5-1ml) per spot, spaced approximately 1cm apart.

Efficacy: Highly effective fat breakdown for submental and lower facial definition, leading to noticeable V-line effect and reduced appearance of double chin.

Due to the delicate nature of facial areas, strict adherence to recommended volumes and injection depths is essential. Post-treatment swelling is common and resolves over several days.

Body Contouring Strategy

Target Areas: Abdomen, love handles (flanks), inner/outer thighs, back rolls, underarm fat

Application Method: Local injection into subcutaneous fat layer using slightly longer gauge needle for deeper body fat layers. Larger volumes administered per session, spread evenly with 1-2cm spacing.

Efficacy: Promotes broad area cellulite reduction and volume loss in stubborn body fat pockets, supporting smoother and more contoured body profile.

Combining treatment with healthy diet and lymphatic massage post-treatment can enhance elimination of released fat. Multiple sessions typically required for optimal body sculpting.

Precautions & Safety Information

Professional Use Only — This product is intended for administration by qualified healthcare professionals only.

Contraindications

  • Pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Blood clotting disorders
  • Known allergies to any ingredients
  • Active infection at treatment site
  • Severe underlying medical conditions

Common Side Effects

  • Swelling at injection site (temporary)
  • Redness and bruising
  • Tenderness and discomfort
  • Minor edema (resolves naturally)

These effects are temporary and typically resolve within several days to weeks.

Storage Conditions
Store in a cool, dark place between 1°C and 30°C. Do not freeze. Keep away from direct sunlight. Once opened, use vial contents within the timeframe specified by manufacturer guidelines to ensure sterility and potency.
Treatment Intervals
Do not administer follow-up treatments sooner than 7 days after the previous session. A 10-14 day interval is often preferred to allow the body to process lipolysis and swelling adequately.
