Key Ingredients & Formulation

20-24mg/ml
Highly purified, cross-linked hyaluronic acid providing excellent volume restoration and hydration. DERMALAX Plus contains 20mg/ml while Deep Plus and Implant Plus contain 24mg/ml for enhanced volumizing effects.
Lidocaine
0.3%
Local anesthetic integrated into the formulation to minimize discomfort during injection procedures, ensuring a more comfortable patient experience.
BDDE Cross-Linking Technology
Advanced low-temperature cross-linking process using BDDE (1,4-Butanediol diglycidyl ether) ensures stable, monophasic gel structure with optimal durability and smooth injection characteristics.
Monophasic Gel Structure
Uniform gel consistency provides smooth and even injection force, resulting in natural-looking results with consistent distribution throughout the treatment area.
Non-Animal Origin
Sourced from non-animal origins through bacterial fermentation, ensuring biocompatibility and reducing the risk of allergic reactions.

Safety Information & Precautions

Important Safety Guidelines
Do not inject intravascularly. Avoid intense heat or cold exposure for 3-4 days post-treatment. This product is for single-use only and should be used by qualified practitioners familiar with injection techniques and facial anatomy.
Post-Treatment Care
Avoid alcohol consumption, sauna sessions, and direct sunbathing for 3-4 days following treatment. Do not combine with other dermal fillers in the same treatment session. Monitor treated area for any unusual reactions.
Storage Requirements
Store at 2-25°C in original packaging. Protect from direct sunlight and freezing temperatures. Check expiration date before use.
Contraindications
Not suitable for patients with known hypersensitivity to hyaluronic acid or lidocaine. Avoid use in areas with active inflammation or infection.
Professional Use Only
This product should only be administered by licensed healthcare professionals with appropriate training in dermal filler injection techniques.
