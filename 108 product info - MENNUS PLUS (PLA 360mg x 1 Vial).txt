## 0. Product Name: MENNUS PLUS (PLA 360mg x 1 Vial)
- MENNUS PLUS is a single-product solution. It is a poly-L-lactic acid (PLLA) based filler, a type of biostimulator that promotes collagen synthesis. The product is provided as a powder in a single vial, requiring reconstitution before use.
- The package contains 1 vial with 360mg of PLA powder.

## 1. Key Ingredients & Concentration
- This product is primarily composed of **Poly-L-lactic Acid (PLLA)**. It contains 360mg of PLA powder per vial.
- The product may be mixed with 8-10ml of sterile distilled water and 1-2ml of 2% Lidocaine for preparation.
- PLLA is a biodegradable and biocompatible polymer that stimulates the body's natural collagen production.

## 2. Efficacy Benefits
- Long-lasting collagen regeneration and volume restoration.
- Improvement of deep wrinkles and fine lines.
- Overall skin rejuvenation and increased elasticity.

## 3. Protocol (Instructions)
- Injection Layer by Treatment Area: Superficial dermal to subcutaneous layer, depending on the area.
- Recommended Needle Gauge: Use an 18G needle to withdraw the solution. A 26G needle or 25G cannula is recommended for injection.
- Treatment Cycle: 1-3 sessions are typically recommended, depending on individual needs. The effects can last for 18-24 months.
