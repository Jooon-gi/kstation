Key Ingredients & Mechanism

[Ingredient mechanism diagram placeholder]
Deoxycholic Acid
A bile acid naturally produced in the body that aids in the breakdown and absorption of dietary fat. Acts as the primary lipolytic agent by destroying fat cell membranes.
Phosphatidylcholine (PPC)
Works synergistically with Deoxycholic Acid to emulsify and break down fat cells, enhancing the overall lipolytic effect.
L-Carnitine
Helps transport fatty acids into the mitochondria to be converted into energy, assisting in the efficient removal of broken-down fat.
Additional Components
Horse Chestnut Extract for microcirculation and swelling reduction, plus vitamins and peptides for skin firming and regeneration.

How It Works

The synergistic action of Deoxycholic Acid and PPC causes the destruction of fat cell membranes (lipolysis). The breakdown products are then naturally metabolized and excreted from the body, with L-Carnitine promoting efficient use of released fatty acids as energy. This process leads to targeted fat reduction and improved contouring, complemented by ingredients that reduce swelling and promote lymphatic drainage.

Usage Protocol & Treatment Guide

[Treatment area mapping image placeholder - Face and body zones]

Treatment Areas

Face: Double chin, submental area, cheeks, jawline, marionette fat
Body: Abdomen, hips, inner/outer thighs, flanks, upper arms, bra line

Recommended Treatment Cycle

Typically 2-3 sessions are required for noticeable results, administered at 1-2 week intervals. The number of sessions can vary based on the target area and the amount of fat to be addressed.

Application Method

Direct injection into the subcutaneous fat layer using a sterile syringe (e.g., 27G or 30G needle) or via mesogun/hyaluron pen. Consult a professional protocol for exact depth and technique. Injection points should be small (0.2-0.5ml for facial, larger volumes for body) and spread across the target zone.

Post-Treatment Care

  • Results become visible gradually as the body naturally eliminates destroyed fat cells
  • Post-treatment massage can aid in lymphatic drainage
  • Avoid vigorous exercise, saunas, and hot tubs for 48 hours
  • Maintain proper hydration to support fat metabolism
  • Ensure sterile technique for all injections

Precautions & Safety Information

Important Safety Information
This product is for professional use only. Proper training and certification in injection techniques are required.
  • Adhere strictly to the recommended 1 to 2-week interval between sessions
  • Allow the body sufficient time to metabolize and excrete broken-down fat cells
  • Monitor for swelling subsidence between treatments
  • Do not exceed recommended dosage per session

Contraindications

Do NOT use on:
  • Pregnant or breastfeeding individuals
  • Those with blood disorders or clotting issues
  • Individuals with allergies to any ingredients
  • Patients with severe liver or kidney disease
  • Active infections or inflammation in treatment area
Do NOT inject:
  • Directly into muscle tissue
  • Into veins or blood vessels
  • Directly into the skin dermis layer

Storage Conditions

Store sealed vials in a cool, dark place, away from direct sunlight. Once opened, any unused contents should be discarded to maintain sterility. Keep out of reach of children.

Expected Side Effects

Common temporary side effects may include mild swelling, redness, bruising, or tenderness at injection sites. These typically subside within 3-7 days. Body treatments may experience more noticeable swelling than facial treatments.
