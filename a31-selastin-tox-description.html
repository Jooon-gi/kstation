‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How soon can I see results with SELASTIN TOX GF11?
The tox-like peptide effects provide immediate muscle relaxation and wrinkle softening within days. Progressive improvements in skin texture, brightness, and elasticity develop over the course of the treatment series (3-5 sessions), with optimal results visible after completing the full protocol.
What makes this different from traditional botulinum toxin treatments?
SELASTIN TOX GF11 uses peptides to provide a tox-like effect without injections into muscles. It offers the additional benefits of collagen stimulation, skin brightening, and cellular regeneration through the 11 Growth Factors, providing comprehensive anti-aging benefits in one treatment.
Is this treatment painful?
The treatment is performed using professional micro-needling devices at shallow depths (0.2-1.5mm). Most patients experience minimal discomfort, which can be further reduced with topical numbing agents if needed. The procedure is well-tolerated by most individuals.
How long do the results last?
Results vary by individual, but the tox-like effects typically last several months. The collagen-stimulating and regenerative effects provide longer-lasting improvements that continue to develop over time. Maintenance treatments every 3-6 months help sustain optimal results.
Can SELASTIN TOX GF11 be combined with other treatments?
Yes, this solution can be integrated into comprehensive skincare protocols. However, combination treatments should always be planned and supervised by qualified professionals to ensure safety and optimal results. Proper spacing between different treatments may be recommended.
Who should not use this product?
Individuals with active skin infections, open wounds, or severe skin conditions should not receive treatment. Pregnant or nursing women should postpone treatment. Always disclose medical conditions and medications to your practitioner for proper assessment.
