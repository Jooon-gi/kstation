How It Works

Miracle Touch BR works by inhibiting melanin transfer to the skin's surface, resulting in a visibly lighter and more uniform skin tone. The synergistic blend of peptides ensures skin regeneration, while hyaluronic acid maintains optimal moisture levels. Niacinamide brightens the complexion, leading to a healthier-looking, illuminated appearance with enhanced light-reflecting properties.

Treatment Protocol & Application

Recommended Treatment Cycle

Initial Treatment: 3 to 5 sessions administered at 1 to 2-week intervals

Maintenance: Follow-up treatments every 2 to 3 months to maintain optimal results

Treatment Areas

  • Entire face (for overall brightening and tone correction)
  • Neck and décolleté
  • Back of hands
  • Targeted areas with uneven tone, melasma, sun damage, or post-acne marks

Application Methods

Professional Application Options:

  • Mesotherapy Injection: Precise application into superficial or papillary dermis for targeted pigmentation treatment (recommended for best results)
  • Microneedling Devices: MTS (Microneedle Therapy System), derma roller, or derma pen for enhanced absorption
  • Nano-Needles: For topical absorption with minimal invasiveness

Note: Professional application technique is highly recommended for optimal results.

Precautions & Post-Treatment Care

Important Safety Information

Please read the following precautions carefully before use:
  • Do not use on broken skin, open wounds, or areas with active infections
  • Contraindicated for individuals with known allergies to any product components
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Use immediately after opening to maintain product sterility

Post-Treatment Care Guidelines

Sun Protection (Critical): Strictly avoid direct sun exposure following treatment. Apply high-SPF sunscreen (SPF 30+) daily to maintain results and prevent hyperpigmentation or reversal of brightening effects.

Skincare Routine: Use gentle, non-irritating products for at least 24-48 hours post-treatment. Avoid active ingredients like retinoids or acids immediately after application.

Hydration: Maintain adequate skin hydration with recommended moisturizers to support the treatment's efficacy.
