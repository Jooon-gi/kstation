Technology

Unique Biphasic Formulation

The Soonsu Shining Peel features a revolutionary two-phase solution that combines the benefits of both oil and water layers for optimal skin treatment results.

Oil Phase
Contains active substances like Squalene to prevent oxidation, protect the skin, and form a moisture barrier. This layer stabilizes the peel's components and significantly reduces the likelihood of side reactions and excessive peeling.
Water Phase
Contains exfoliating acids and vitamins to stimulate dermal cell renewal, promoting deep skin regeneration and brightening effects.

Application Guide

Usage Protocol (Treatment Guide)

Recommended Cycle
4-6 sessions, every 7-12 days (1-2 week intervals)
Treatment Areas
Face, Neck, Décolleté, Hands, Elbows, Groin, Stretch Mark Areas
1
Preparation
Remove the cap and shake the vial thoroughly for a few seconds until the two layers fully mix into a temporary emulsion, often indicated by a color change (e.g., to temporary green/yellow).
2
Extraction
Using a syringe (e.g., 17G or 18G drawing needle), extract the required dosage (typically 1.5-3ml depending on the area).
3
Application
Apply the solution using nitrile gloves or a brush, massaging vigorously into the skin in circular motions, starting from the T-zone and moving to the U-zone. Avoid contact with the eyes.
4
Duration
Allow the product to perform for a specific time based on the area (see treatment strategy table below).
5
Removal
Cleanse the area with a gauze or non-woven pad soaked in plain water.
6
Post-Care
Follow immediately with a neutralizing or glutathione-based SPF 50+ product for optimal protection and results.
