Storage & Safety Information

Professional Use Only – These products are designed for professional aesthetic and clinical use. Proper training and technique are required for safe administration.

Storage Requirements

  • Store at ambient/cool conditions below 30°C
  • Keep away from direct sunlight, extreme heat, and freezing temperatures
  • Avoid exposure to steam or high humidity
  • Use immediately after opening to maintain sterility and potency
  • Discard any remainder after opening

Safety Precautions

  • For professional (aesthetic/clinical) use only
  • Perform patch test before full application
  • Do not apply over open wounds, irritated, or inflamed skin
  • Avoid direct eye contact
  • Ensure proper injection technique and depth
  • Follow manufacturer protocols for optimal results
