Safety Information & Precautions

Professional Use Only — This product is intended for use by qualified medical professionals with appropriate training in injection techniques.

Important Precautions

  • Do not exceed maximum 50mL (5 vials) per treatment session
  • Consult medical professional before use
  • Avoid use if allergic to any ingredients
  • Not recommended for pregnant or nursing women
  • Maintain proper injection technique and sterility

Contraindications

  • Known hypersensitivity to PPC or DC
  • Active skin infections at treatment site
  • Pregnancy and lactation
  • Severe cardiovascular conditions
  • Blood clotting disorders
