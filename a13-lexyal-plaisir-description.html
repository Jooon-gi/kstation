LEXYAL Plaisir is a hybrid injectable solution classified as a Poly-L-Lactic Acid (PLLA) based collagen stimulator. It comes as a dry powder in a single vial that must be reconstituted with saline and Lidocaine before use. The product line offers two distinct concentrations to address different aesthetic concerns and treatment depths.

Key Ingredients & Composition

Primary Active Components
Poly-L-Lactic Acid (PLLA) 50mg (120mg) / 100mg (240mg)

A biodegradable polymer featuring uniform microspheres that stimulate the body's natural collagen production. PLLA acts as a scaffold that triggers a bio-stimulation response, encouraging gradual Type I and Type III collagen synthesis for long-lasting rejuvenation.

Carboxymethyl Cellulose (CMC)

A stabilizing agent that ensures smooth reconstitution and uniform injection consistency. CMC helps maintain the suspension stability and prevents particle aggregation during the injection process.

Mannitol

Acts as an additional stabilizer to preserve product integrity and facilitate optimal reconstitution. Mannitol ensures the PLLA powder dissolves evenly when mixed with the diluent.

Lidocaine (Added During Reconstitution)

Lidocaine is not included in the dry product vial but is typically added by the practitioner during reconstitution (0.5ml for 120mg, 1ml for 240mg) to enhance patient comfort during the injection procedure.

Formulation Technology

LEXYAL Plaisir utilizes Uniform PLLA Microspheres‚Äîconsistently sized particles designed to minimize the risk of common PLLA side effects such as nodules, clumping, or granulomas. This advanced formulation technology ensures predictable, safe, and natural-looking results.

Treatment Protocol & Application

Reconstitution & Injection Guidelines
1
Product Selection
Choose LEXYAL Plaisir 120mg for superficial rejuvenation and fine lines, or 240mg for deep volume restoration and structural support based on treatment goals and target areas.
2
Reconstitution Preparation
Mix the dry PLLA powder with normal saline and Lidocaine. For 120mg: use 4-6ml saline + 0.5ml Lidocaine. For 240mg: use 7-8ml saline + 1ml Lidocaine.
3
Resting Period
Allow the mixture to rest for approximately 20-30 minutes after initial blending to ensure the PLLA powder is fully dissolved and the mixture is smooth and uniform.
4
Injection Technique
For 120mg: inject into the dermal layer (1-1.2mm depth) using 32G/33G nano needles. For 240mg: inject into the subdermal layer using 23G/25G cannula for deeper volume restoration.
5
Treatment Cycle
Complete 2-3 treatment sessions spaced 4-8 weeks apart, depending on the severity of skin deficiency and desired results. Schedule follow-up sessions as needed for optimal outcomes.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does LEXYAL Plaisir differ from traditional dermal fillers?

Unlike hyaluronic acid fillers that provide immediate volume, LEXYAL Plaisir is a biostimulator that works by triggering your body's natural collagen production. Results develop gradually and last longer (12-18 months vs. 6-12 months for most fillers).

How many treatment sessions will I need?

Most patients require 2-3 treatment sessions spaced 4-8 weeks apart for optimal results. Your practitioner will customize a treatment plan based on your individual needs and aesthetic goals.

When will I see results?

Initial improvements may be subtle. Full results develop gradually over 8-12 weeks as your body produces new collagen. This progressive approach ensures natural-looking enhancement.

Is the treatment painful?

Lidocaine is added during reconstitution to enhance comfort. Most patients report minimal discomfort during the procedure. Additional topical numbing may be applied if needed.

Can I combine LEXYAL Plaisir with other treatments?

Yes, LEXYAL Plaisir can be part of a comprehensive aesthetic treatment plan. Consult with your practitioner about combining with other modalities for enhanced results.

What is the downtime?

Minimal downtime. Most patients experience mild swelling or redness for 1-3 days. You can typically return to normal activities immediately, avoiding strenuous exercise for 24-48 hours.
