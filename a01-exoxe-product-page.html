Usage Protocol
Preparation
Combine 5ml solution (HA for Face/Skin, Saline for Hair) into the vial of lyophilized powder (50mg PURE EXO) using a syringe. Mix gently until uniform liquid is obtained.
Application Method
Professional use via Microneedling (MTS, Derma Pen) at depth 0.2-2.0mm, Mesotherapy/Injector Therapy, or topical application following intensive procedures.
Treatment Cycle
Face/Skin: 3 sessions every 1-2 weeks (2.5-3.0ml per session). Scalp/Hair: Minimum 4 sessions, ideally 4+2, with 5ml per session.
Post-Treatment Care
Apply moisturizing or soothing cream as needed. Avoid intervals exceeding 4 weeks between sessions.

Precautions & Storage
Storage Requirements — Store in a dry place at room temperature (1°C-30°C), away from direct sunlight. After reconstitution, use immediately or refrigerate (1°C-3°C) and use within one week.

General Precautions

  • For external use only by trained professionals
  • Avoid contact with eyes
  • Do not apply to scarred or irritated skin (dermatitis, eczema)
  • Discontinue use and consult professional if skin problems occur

Contraindications

  • Autoimmune or connective tissue diseases
  • Existing facial implants
  • Hypertrophic or keloid scarring tendency
  • Pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Under 18 years of age
Frequently Asked Questions
How long do the effects last?
Effects typically last around 10-11 months for facial treatments. Maintenance treatments recommended 1-2 times per year to prolong results.
What is the difference between Face/Skin and Hair versions?
Face/Skin version is enriched with Fibroblast Growth Factors (FGFs) for dermal regeneration and uses HA solution. Hair version contains Vascular Endothelial Growth Factors (VEGFs) for scalp microcirculation and uses Saline solution to prevent clumping.
Can I restart treatment if I miss the 4-week window?
If the interval between sessions exceeds 4 weeks, the therapy cycle should be restarted from the beginning for optimal results.
What makes exosomes effective for skin regeneration?
Exosomes are nanosized vesicles (100-150nm) that act as cellular communicators, delivering over 1,000 types of growth factors and proteins directly to target cells, triggering natural regeneration processes including collagen synthesis increase up to 600%.
Why use Saline for scalp treatment instead of HA?
Saline solution is recommended for scalp applications to prevent clumping and ensure comfortable, effective delivery to hair follicles.
