Safety Information & Guidelines

Important Safety Guidelines
For professional use only. Ensure proper training before administration. Use sterile technique and follow injection safety protocols. Store according to manufacturer specifications and use before expiration date.
Precautions & Contraindications
Not recommended for pregnant or breastfeeding women. Avoid use in patients with known allergies to salmon or marine proteins. Do not inject into infected or inflamed areas. Consult healthcare provider for patients with autoimmune conditions.
Post-Treatment Care
Gentle massage recommended after injection for even distribution. Avoid excessive heat, sun exposure, and strenuous exercise for 24-48 hours post-treatment. Monitor for any unusual reactions and seek medical attention if needed.
Adverse Reactions
Minimal side effects reported. Temporary mild swelling, redness, or bruising may occur at injection sites. Usually resolves within 24-48 hours.
Professional Requirements
Should be administered by qualified medical professionals with appropriate training in injectable treatments and aesthetic procedures.
Patient Selection
Suitable for most skin types including sensitive skin. Individual assessment recommended to ensure appropriate candidate selection.
