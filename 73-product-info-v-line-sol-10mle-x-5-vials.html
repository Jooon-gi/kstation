APPLICATION PROTOCOL
1
AREA PREPARATION & MARKING
Clean and disinfect treatment area. Mark injection points approximately 1cm apart across the target zone. Ensure sterile environment and proper equipment preparation.
2
INJECTION TECHNIQUE
Inject 0.1-0.2ml per point at 5mm depth into subcutaneous fat layer. Use consistent technique maintaining proper needle angle and injection speed for optimal distribution.
3
POST-INJECTION CARE
Apply gentle massage if required. Monitor for immediate reactions. Maximum 2ml per full face treatment. Avoid water contact for 4 hours minimum.
TREATMENT SCHEDULE
Results visible after first treatment. Complete course consists of 4-6 sessions spaced 2-3 weeks apart for optimal outcomes and progressive improvement.

Professional use only. Follow all application guidelines strictly to ensure safe and effective treatment outcomes.
