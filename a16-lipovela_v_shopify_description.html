FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
HOW DOES LIPOVELA V WORK TO REDUCE FAT?
LIPOVELA V contains Deoxycholic Acid and Phosphatidylcholine (PPC) that work synergistically to destroy fat cell membranes through lipolysis. Once the fat cells are broken down, L-Carnitine facilitates the transport and metabolism of liberated fatty acids, allowing them to be naturally processed and excreted by the body. This results in permanent fat cell destruction and long-lasting contouring results.
WHAT IS THE EXPECTED TIMELINE FOR VISIBLE RESULTS?
Initial results may be visible within 2-4 weeks after the first treatment as the body begins to metabolize destroyed fat cells. Progressive improvements occur with each session. Optimal results are typically achieved 4-6 weeks after completing the full treatment cycle (3-6 sessions). Individual results may vary based on the treatment area, volume of fat, and patient's metabolic rate.
ARE THERE ANY CONTRAINDICATIONS OR LIMITATIONS?
LIPOVELA V should not be used in patients with active infections in the treatment area, bleeding disorders, or allergies to any of the ingredients. Pregnant or breastfeeding women should avoid this treatment. Patients with severe liver or kidney disease should consult with a healthcare professional before treatment. Not recommended for large-volume fat reduction or as a substitute for weight loss.
