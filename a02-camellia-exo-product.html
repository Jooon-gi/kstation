Professional Application Protocol

1
Preparation
Open the vial containing the Exosome Powder (Ampoule 1) and the vial containing the PDRN/Collagen Solvent (Ampoule 2). Carefully remove the rubber stoppers from both vials.
2
Mixing
Carefully pour the entire contents of the PDRN/Solvent Ampoule into the Exosome Powder vial. Mix thoroughly until the powder is completely dissolved and the solution is uniform.
3
Application
Apply the mixed solution directly to the desired treatment area (face, neck, décolletage). For optimal results, use during or immediately after a microneedling session to enhance penetration.
4
Treatment Frequency
Recommended program: Apply once per week for a course of 4 consecutive weeks for optimal regenerative effects and visible results.

Microneedling Enhancement

Ideal for use with MTS rollers or dermapens to significantly enhance skin penetration and effectiveness. The microneedling process creates microchannels that allow the exosomes and active ingredients to reach deeper skin layers for maximum efficacy.

Storage Instructions

Store unmixed, sealed vials in a cool, dark place or refrigerated as specified on the packaging to preserve ingredient integrity and potency. Keep away from direct sunlight and heat sources. Do not freeze.

Immediate Use Required

The mixed solution must be used immediately after preparation to maintain the stability and potency of the active components, particularly the delicate exosomes. Do not prepare in advance or attempt to store mixed solution.

What makes CAMELLIA EXO N°5 different from other skin boosters?

CAMELLIA EXO N°5 uniquely combines 5 plant-derived exosomes with high-concentration PDRN (30,000 ppm) and a specialized collagen-peptide complex. This triple-action formula provides comprehensive regeneration at the cellular level, going beyond surface hydration to promote true skin renewal.

Initial improvements in skin texture and hydration can be noticed within days after the first treatment. Optimal results appear after completing the recommended 4-week program (once weekly), with continued improvement in elasticity, firmness, and overall skin quality developing over time.

Can it be used without microneedling?

Why is the dual ampoule system important?

The dual ampoule system separates the delicate lyophilized exosome powder from the liquid PDRN/collagen solvent until the moment of use. This preserves maximum ingredient stability, potency, and efficacy, ensuring you receive the full therapeutic benefits of fresh, active exosomes.
