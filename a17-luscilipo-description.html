Recommended Treatment Cycle
A series of 3-6 sessions is typically recommended, with an interval of 1 to 2 weeks between each session. Visible results often become apparent after the 2nd or 3rd session as the body progressively metabolizes the dissolved fat.
Application Method
Localized injection into the subcutaneous fat layer using a sterile syringe (27G or 30G needle recommended). Inject small, consistent amounts (0.2-0.3 ml) per injection point, maintaining approximately 1 cm spacing between injection sites. Do not exceed recommended volume limits per session.

Precautions & Storage Guidelines
Storage Conditions
Store unopened vials in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Recommended storage temperature: 1°C to 30°C. Once opened, handle remaining solution according to professional guidelines or discard appropriately. Do not freeze or expose to extreme temperatures.
Treatment Interval Guidelines
Maintain the recommended 7-to-14-day interval between sessions in the same treatment area. This allows sufficient time for the body to metabolize and clear the broken-down fat through the lymphatic system and enables proper healing of injection sites.
Post-Treatment Care
  • Perform gentle massage on the treated area to promote even distribution and lymphatic drainage
  • Maintain adequate hydration (increase water intake) to assist the body's natural detoxification processes
  • Avoid strenuous exercise for 24-48 hours post-treatment
  • Avoid alcohol consumption for 24 hours after treatment
  • Apply cold compress if swelling occurs (Bromelain already minimizes this effect)
  • Monitor treated areas for any unusual reactions and consult healthcare provider if concerns arise
Important Safety Information

Not recommended for pregnant or nursing women, individuals with active infections at treatment sites, or those with known allergies to any ingredients. Consult with a healthcare provider before treatment if you have any medical conditions or concerns.

Frequently Asked Questions
How does LusciLipo differ from other fat dissolving injections?
LusciLipo features a unique combination of Lecithin for fat dissolution and Bromelain for anti-inflammatory effects. The inclusion of Bromelain is a key differentiator, as it actively manages post-injection inflammation and swelling, resulting in reduced downtime and improved patient comfort compared to standard fat dissolving solutions.
When can I expect to see results?
Visible results typically become apparent after the 2nd or 3rd treatment session, as the body progressively metabolizes the dissolved fat through the lymphatic system. Full results are usually achieved within 4-8 weeks after completing the recommended 3-6 session series, though individual response times may vary based on treatment area, metabolism, and lifestyle factors.
Is the treatment painful?
Discomfort during treatment is typically minimal to moderate and varies by individual pain tolerance and treatment area. Most patients describe a burning or stinging sensation during injection that subsides within minutes. Topical anesthetic can be applied before treatment to enhance comfort. The Bromelain component helps reduce post-treatment discomfort and swelling.
Are the results permanent?
When fat cells are broken down and eliminated through LusciLipo treatment, they do not regenerate. However, maintaining results requires a healthy lifestyle with proper diet and exercise. Significant weight gain after treatment can cause remaining fat cells to enlarge, potentially diminishing the contouring effects achieved.
What are the common side effects?
Common side effects include temporary swelling, redness, bruising, and tenderness at injection sites. These typically resolve within 3-7 days. The Bromelain component in LusciLipo is specifically included to minimize these effects. Rare side effects may include temporary numbness, itching, or firmness in treated areas. Serious adverse reactions are uncommon when administered by qualified professionals.
Who should not use LusciLipo?
LusciLipo is not recommended for pregnant or nursing women, individuals with active infections or inflammation at treatment sites, those with known allergies to any ingredients (especially bromelain or pineapple), people with severe liver or kidney disease, or those with bleeding disorders. A thorough consultation with a qualified healthcare provider is essential to determine treatment suitability.
