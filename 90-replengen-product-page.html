Advanced Technology Features

HAMC Technology

Hyaluronic Acid Moisture Correction technology delays HA decomposition in the body for longer-lasting results and enhanced durability.

GI (Grinding Irregular) Technology

Creates fine-tuned, homogeneous particles ensuring smooth injection and stable operation for optimal treatment experience.

BDDE Cross-linking

Advanced cross-linking method provides optimal gel stability and longevity while maintaining natural tissue integration.

Premium Raw Materials

Sourced from Shiseido Company, Japan, ensuring highest quality hyaluronic acid for superior safety and efficacy.
