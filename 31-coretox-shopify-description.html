Application Guide

Professional Usage Protocol

1

Preparation & Reconstitution

Reconstitute with sterile, non-preserved saline (0.9% Sodium Chloride). Gently swirl the vial to dissolve the powder, avoiding vigorous shaking to maintain product integrity.
2

Dosage Determination

Determine appropriate dosage based on treatment area and individual patient needs. Common applications include glabella, forehead, and masseter muscles with area-specific unit recommendations.
3

Injection Technique

Administer via precise intramuscular injections using proper aseptic techniques. Follow anatomical injection points to ensure optimal results while avoiding surrounding muscle groups.
4

Post-Treatment Care

Advise patients to avoid massaging treated areas and strenuous exercise for at least 24 hours. Monitor for onset of effects within 2-7 days post-treatment.

Important Safety Information

Professional Use Only: This product must be administered by qualified medical professionals only.

Contraindications: Do not use on pregnant or breastfeeding individuals, or those with known hypersensitivity to botulinum toxin products.

Storage Requirements: Store unopened vials in refrigerator at 2-8°C. Use reconstituted solution immediately.

Post-Treatment Care: Patients should avoid massaging treated areas and strenuous exercise for 24 hours following injection.
