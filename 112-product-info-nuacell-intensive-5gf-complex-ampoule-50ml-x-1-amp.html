Safety Information
Patch Test Recommended – Perform a patch test before first use. Apply a small amount to inner wrist or behind ear and wait 24 hours.
Discontinue Use If – Stop using immediately if redness, swelling, or irritation occurs and consult a dermatologist if symptoms persist.
For External Use Only – Avoid contact with eyes. If contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water.
Frequently Asked Questions
How often should I use the 5GF Complex Ampoule?
The ampoule is designed for daily use as part of your skincare routine. You can use it once or twice daily, depending on your skin's needs and tolerance.
Can I use this with other skincare products?
Yes, this ampoule can be incorporated into your existing skincare routine. Apply after cleansing and toning, but before heavier creams or oils.
What makes the 5GF complex special?
The 5GF complex contains five different growth factors (EGF, HGF, FGF, TGF, and Copper Tripeptide-1) that work synergistically to support skin regeneration and improve elasticity.
How long before I see results?
Individual results may vary, but many users notice improvements in skin texture and hydration within 2-4 weeks of consistent daily use.
