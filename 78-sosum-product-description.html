SOSUM H Protocol

  • Deep dermis to subcutaneous injection
  • Facial contouring and volumization

  • Larger needle gauge (25G-27G) required

Safety & Precautions

Professional Use Only — This product is intended for use by qualified medical professionals trained in dermal filler injection techniques.

Post-Treatment Care

  • Avoid touching treated area for 24 hours
  • Refrain from alcohol and intense exercise for 48 hours
  • Avoid extreme temperatures for 48 hours
  • No sun exposure for 14 days post-treatment

Storage Guidelines

  • Room temperature storage (1°C to 30°C)
  • Do not freeze or overheat
  • Keep in original packaging
  • Check expiration date before use

Product Selection Guide

Choose the appropriate SOSUM variant based on treatment objectives and anatomical considerations

Selection Criteria

SOSUM S
For fine lines around eyes, subtle lip enhancement, and delicate areas requiring minimal volume. Small particle size ensures smooth, even distribution for natural hydration effects.
SOSUM M
For moderate wrinkles like nasolabial folds and marionette lines. Medium particle size provides balanced volume and natural integration for versatile corrections.
SOSUM H
For structural volumization and contouring of chin, jawline, and nose. Large particle size and high viscosity offer significant lifting capacity and architectural support.
