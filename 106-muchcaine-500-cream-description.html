Professional Application Guide

1
Prepare Treatment Area
Clean and dry the treatment area thoroughly before application. Ensure no oils, lotions, or debris are present on the skin surface.
2
Apply Thick Layer
Apply a thick layer of cream to cover the treatment area completely. Maximum recommended dose for adults is 0.5g per application. Do not apply to mucous membranes or damaged skin.
3
Optional Occlusion
A dressing or wrap may be used to enhance absorption if needed for deeper penetration, though this is optional for most procedures.
4
Wait for Anesthetic Effect
Allow 15 minutes to 1 hour depending on the procedure type and patient sensitivity. For laser treatments: 15-30 minutes. For tattooing: 30-60 minutes.
5
Remove Before Procedure
Wipe off the cream completely just before beginning the procedure. The numbing effect will remain active for the treatment duration.

Important Safety Information
Do not use on people with known sensitivity to lidocaine or other amide preparations. Do not apply to open wounds, inflamed, or broken skin. Avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes. Consult a doctor if allergic reaction or severe irritation occurs. Use with caution in patients with liver or kidney disease. Keep out of reach of children.
