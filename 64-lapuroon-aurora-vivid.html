Safety & Precautions
Professional Use Only — This product is intended for use by qualified medical practitioners. Proper training and certification required.
Contraindications — Do not use if allergic to any ingredients. Not suitable for areas with active infections, acne, or inflammation. Pregnant or breastfeeding individuals should consult medical professional.
Expected Side Effects — Mild redness, swelling, or bruising may occur at injection sites and typically resolves within 2-3 days.
Post-Treatment Care — Avoid direct sun exposure, strenuous activity, saunas, and hot baths for 24-48 hours. Keep treated area clean and moisturized.
