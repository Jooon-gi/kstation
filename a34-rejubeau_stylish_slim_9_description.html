Q. How does Rejubeau Stylish Slim 9 differ from other fat dissolving solutions?

Q. Is the fat reduction permanent?

A. Yes, the treatment causes permanent destruction of fat cells in the treated area. However, maintaining results requires a stable weight, as remaining fat cells can still enlarge with weight gain.

A. Most patients require 3-6 sessions depending on the treatment area and individual fat layer thickness. Facial areas typically need fewer sessions than larger body areas.

A. Patients can resume normal activities immediately, though swelling and tenderness may persist for 3-7 days. Strenuous exercise should be avoided for 24-48 hours post-treatment.

A. No, Rejubeau Stylish Slim 9 is designed for targeted reduction of localized fat deposits, not general weight loss. It is most effective for contouring specific areas resistant to diet and exercise.

Q. What areas respond best to treatment?

A. The double chin, jawline, and submental area typically show excellent results. Body areas like upper arms, abdomen, love handles, and inner thighs also respond well to treatment.

Q. Are there any long-term side effects?

A. When administered properly by qualified professionals, long-term side effects are rare. The most common effects are temporary swelling and tenderness at injection sites.
