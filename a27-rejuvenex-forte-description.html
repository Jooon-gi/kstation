Specialized Formulations

Rejuvenex Vega Shine

Formulation Highlights

c-PDRN combined with 3-O-ethyl ascorbic acid (stable Vitamin C), powerful antioxidant Glutathione, and Sodium Hyaluronate for comprehensive brightening.

A specialized brightening booster that targets oxidative stress and pigmentation. Glutathione supports detoxification and whitening processes, while c-PDRN and HA provide hydration and cell renewal for a visibly radiant, dewy complexion. Formulated to combat dullness and environmental damage, restoring vitality and illumination to stressed skin.

Application Method: Can be applied via MTS/Microneedling or shallow injection techniques for optimal ingredient delivery.

Rejuvenex Hydro Essence

Formulation Highlights

c-PDRN and non-crosslinked high-purity Sodium Hyaluronate for intensive moisture restoration.

Ideal for severely dry, dehydrated, or sensitive skin types. Focuses on deep moisture retention and strengthening the skin's protective barrier. The combination offers rapid hydration and helps smooth superficial fine lines caused by dryness, making it excellent for pre- and post-procedure recovery. Specifically designed to create a long-lasting internal moisture reservoir in the dermis.

Application Method: Shallow injection or microneedling ensures HA and c-PDRN are optimally delivered for hydro-rejuvenation.

Rejuvenex Sculpt V

Formulation Highlights

c-PDRN blended with lipolytic agents including Sodium Deoxycholate, L-Carnitine, and Lecithin, complemented by soothing botanical extracts.

A contouring solution designed to promote localized fat breakdown (lipolysis) and reduce fluid retention (edema), while the c-PDRN simultaneously ensures skin tightness and elasticity are maintained during the slimming process. Helps achieve a more defined and sculpted facial or body contour without invasive procedures.

Application Method: Targeted injection into localized fat deposits for precise contouring effects.
