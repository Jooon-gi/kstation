Product Overview

Hyaluronic Acid Filler Pre-filled Syringe HICE Technology

YVOIRE fillers contain highly cross-linked Hyaluronic Acid of non-animal origin with Lidocaine for enhanced comfort. Each product is supplied in a single-use package containing one 1.0 ml pre-filled syringe with accompanying needles.

Duration: 6-12 months with natural, long-lasting results

Key Components

Hyaluronic Acid
Non-animal origin
22 mg/ml concentration with HICE (High Concentration Equalized) cross-linking technology for stable gel structure and minimal BDDE cross-linkers.
Lidocaine Hydrochloride
Local anesthetic
0.3% concentration provides enhanced comfort during injection procedures, reducing patient discomfort and improving treatment experience.

Detailed Product Information

YVOIRE Classic Plus
Low to Medium Viscosity
Designed for correction of fine and shallow lines, skin rejuvenation, and lip augmentation. Highly elastic with excellent flowability for natural results. The typical duration of effect is approximately 6-12 months.
Treatment Areas: Glabella lines, tear troughs, crow's feet, barcode lines, upper eyelids.
YVOIRE Volume Plus
Medium to High Viscosity
Higher viscosity and viscoelasticity provide strong support and lifting capacity for deep facial depressions and volume restoration. The typical duration of effect is approximately 6-12 months.
Treatment Areas: Nasolabial folds, marionette lines, cheeks, temples, forehead, nose and lip augmentation.
YVOIRE Contour Plus
High Viscosity
Highest viscosity and elasticity providing exceptional resistance to deformation for long-lasting, firm facial structure definition. The typical duration of effect is approximately 6-12 months.
Treatment Areas: Cheekbones, chin, jawline, facial lipoatrophy correction, morphological deformities.

Important Safety Information
Do not use in individuals with known hypersensitivity to hyaluronic acid or lidocaine. Consult healthcare provider before treatment.

Storage & Precautions

Precautions

  • Do not use in individuals with known hypersensitivity to hyaluronic acid or lidocaine
  • Inform healthcare provider of any medical conditions or medications before use
  • Treatment should only be performed by qualified medical professionals
  • Follow proper injection techniques and sterile procedures

Storage Conditions

  • Store at a temperature between 2°C and 25°C
  • Do not freeze the product
  • This product is for single use only
  • Keep in original packaging until use

For Healthcare Professionals

Injection Techniques

  • Use appropriate needle gauge as recommended for each product variant
  • Follow sterile injection procedures and aseptic techniques
  • Consider patient's facial anatomy and tissue characteristics
  • Inject slowly and steadily for optimal distribution
  • Massage gently if needed to ensure even distribution

Treatment Protocols

  • Assess patient suitability and medical history
  • Perform allergy testing if indicated
  • Plan treatment areas and volumes based on desired outcomes
  • Schedule follow-up appointments for assessment
  • Document treatment details and patient response
