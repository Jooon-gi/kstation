Safety & Precautions
Storage Requirements — Store at 1-30°C. Do not freeze. Single-use product, use immediately after opening.
Contraindications — Do not use on inflamed or infected skin areas. Not recommended during pregnancy or nursing.
Post-Treatment Care — Avoid strenuous activities, extreme temperatures (saunas, tanning beds), and alcohol consumption for 48 hours post-treatment.

Frequently Asked Questions
How long do ELRAVIE PREMIER results last?
The aesthetic effect typically lasts between 9-12 months. With a single re-touch treatment, the duration can be extended up to 18 months, depending on individual factors and treatment area.
Which version should I choose for my treatment?
Choose LIGHT for fine lines and superficial wrinkles, DEEP for medium to deep wrinkles and nasolabial folds, and VOLUME for significant volume enhancement and facial contouring. Your practitioner will recommend the best option based on your specific needs.
What makes ELRAVIE PREMIER different from other fillers?
ELRAVIE PREMIER features advanced HI (High molecular Ideal Reticulated Matrix) technology with monophasic gel structure, providing enhanced stability, cohesiveness, and reduced residual BDDE for superior safety and effectiveness.
Is the treatment painful?
ELRAVIE PREMIER contains 0.3% lidocaine for pain relief during injection. Most patients experience minimal discomfort during the treatment procedure.
What should I expect after treatment?
Mild swelling, redness, or bruising at the injection site is common and typically resolves within 48 hours. Follow post-treatment care instructions provided by your practitioner for optimal results.
