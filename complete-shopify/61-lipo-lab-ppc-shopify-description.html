Professional Application Protocol

1
Area Preparation
Clean and disinfect the treatment area thoroughly. Mark injection points approximately 1cm apart across the target fat deposit area for optimal coverage and results.
2
Injection Technique
Inject 0.2-0.4ml per point directly into the subcutaneous fat layer. Use proper injection technique to ensure even distribution and minimize patient discomfort.
3
Post-Treatment Care
Apply gentle pressure to injection sites and provide aftercare instructions. Patients may experience temporary swelling, bruising, or redness at injection sites.
4
Follow-up Schedule
Schedule next session 10-14 days after treatment. Complete treatment cycle typically requires 4-6 sessions for optimal fat reduction results.

Mechanism of Action

Lipolytic Process

The PPC component works to destroy fat cell membranes through phospholipase activation, while Sodium Deoxycholate helps emulsify the released fat. The body then naturally processes and eliminates the broken-down fat cells through the lymphatic system and metabolism.

Enhanced Metabolism

L-Carnitine accelerates the fat metabolism process, helping the body more efficiently convert released fatty acids into energy and facilitating faster elimination of destroyed fat cells.
