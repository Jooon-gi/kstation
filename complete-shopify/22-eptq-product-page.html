Safety Information

Professional Use Only — This product must be administered by licensed medical professionals with appropriate training in dermal filler injection techniques.

Contraindications

  • History of severe allergies or anaphylaxis
  • Pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Active skin infection at injection site
  • Autoimmune disorders affecting connective tissue

Important Precautions

  • Do not inject into blood vessels
  • Avoid use in areas with permanent implants
  • Monitor for signs of allergic reaction
  • Follow proper injection techniques

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do the results last? +
Results typically last 6-12 months for S100 and S300, and 12-18 months for S500, depending on treatment area, skin type, and individual metabolism.
What makes E.P.T.Q. different from other fillers? +
E.P.T.Q. features patented HIVE-structure technology and BDDE cross-linking for superior elasticity, shape retention, and natural-looking results with integrated lidocaine for enhanced comfort.
Which variant should I choose for my treatment? +
S100 for fine lines and delicate areas, S300 for moderate wrinkles and lip enhancement, S500 for deep volume restoration and facial contouring. Consult with your practitioner for personalized recommendations.
