Safety Information & Precautions

Professional Use Only – This product is intended for use by qualified medical professionals experienced in dermal filler injection techniques.

General Precautions

  • Avoid touching or massaging treated area for 24-48 hours post-injection
  • Refrain from intense physical activity for at least 48 hours
  • Avoid alcohol consumption and extreme temperature exposure initially
  • Use cold compresses if swelling or bruising occurs

Emergency Considerations

  1. Monitor for signs of adverse reactions
  2. Ensure emergency protocols are readily available
  3. Maintain appropriate reversal agents if clinically indicated
  4. Provide clear post-treatment contact information
