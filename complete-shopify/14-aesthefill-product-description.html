Treatment Protocol & Application Guide

Injection Layer & Depth
Deep dermis or subcutaneous layer injection recommended. Injection depth can be varied based on specific treatment area and desired effect for optimal results.
Needle Specifications
Sterile 25G or 26G cannula needle recommended for injection. Proper needle selection ensures optimal product delivery and patient comfort.
Treatment Cycle
Complete treatment of 1-3 sessions recommended for optimal results. Maintain 4-6 week intervals between each session for proper tissue response.
Reconstitution Guidelines
Reconstitute with sterile water to form suspension for injection. Concentration can be customized during reconstitution to suit different treatment areas.
Volume Strategy Applications
Thick suspension for temples, malar region, nasolabial folds, jawline, and chin. Inject into deep dermis or subcutaneous layer using 25G-26G cannula.
Fine Line Strategy Applications
Thin suspension for eye area, forehead, and neck lines. Inject into superficial dermis with finer gauge for subtle improvements.
Single-Use Protocol
Product is for single-use after reconstitution. Must be used immediately after preparation to maintain sterility and effectiveness.

Safety Information & Precautions

Important Precautions
Avoid strenuous activity, saunas, and direct sun exposure for 48 hours post-treatment. Do not receive injections if pregnant, breastfeeding, or if there is an active infection or inflammation at the injection site.
Storage Requirements
Store product at stable temperature away from direct sunlight. Product is for single-use after reconstitution and should be used immediately after preparation to maintain sterility and effectiveness.
Post-Treatment Care
Follow recommended aftercare procedures for optimal results. Avoid excessive facial movements and maintain proper hygiene at injection sites.
Contraindications
Not suitable for pregnant or breastfeeding women. Avoid treatment in areas with active infection, inflammation, or compromised skin integrity.
Professional Administration
Treatment should only be performed by qualified medical professionals with appropriate training in dermal filler and biostimulator injection techniques.
