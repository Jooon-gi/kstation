Product Details

Detailed Product Versions

STARFILL PLUS Lidocaine

Designed for the correction of fine, superficial facial lines and wrinkles. It is ideal for subtle enhancements. Its low-medium viscosity and uniform gel texture make it suitable for delicate areas, providing a smooth and natural finish.

STARFILL DEEP PLUS Lidocaine

Targets moderately pronounced and deep facial lines and wrinkles. It is also suitable for lip augmentation and contouring. This version has a balanced viscosity that provides both structure and flexibility, making it versatile for treating a range of moderate to deep lines and folds.

STARFILL IMPLANT PLUS Lidocaine

Used for severe lines and folds, as well as for facial volumization and contouring. It can also be used for nose bridge improvement. With the highest viscosity in the series, STARFILL IMPLANT PLUS provides the necessary structure and lifting capacity for profound volume enhancement and the correction of severe facial depressions.
