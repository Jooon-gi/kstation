Important Safety Information

Professional medical supervision required for administration. This product is designed for dispensing single doses to multiple patients in clinical settings.

  • Must be diluted before administration - never inject undiluted
  • Complete all dispensing within 4 hours of first vial puncture
  • Contraindicated in patients with kidney stone history
  • Use caution in patients with G6PD deficiency
  • May interfere with glucose testing - inform laboratory staff
  • Consult healthcare provider if pregnant or breastfeeding
