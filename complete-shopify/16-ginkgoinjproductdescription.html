Administration Protocols

Standard Treatment Protocol

Daily intramuscular or intravenous injection of 5-10mL, or intravenous injection of 15mL every two days as determined by healthcare professional.

Acute Treatment Protocol

For acute conditions: 25mL daily by intravenous drip. For severe cases: 25-50mL daily by intravenous injection or IV drip.

Safety Information & Storage

Important Safety Information
Professional use only. This product requires careful handling and administration by qualified healthcare providers.
  • Not for use in pregnant or breastfeeding women
  • Contraindicated with bleeding disorders
  • Avoid concurrent use with anticoagulants
  • Single-use ampoules - discard after opening
  • Do not consume raw ginkgo seeds

Storage Requirements

Store in sealed container, protected from light, at room temperature (1-30°C). Each ampoule is intended for single use and any remaining solution should be discarded after opening.

Contraindications

Do not use in patients with known hypersensitivity to ingredients, bleeding disorders, or increased bleeding risk. Use with caution in patients taking anticoagulant or antiplatelet medications.
