Application Guidelines
1
Preparation Phase
Prepare treatment area with proper cleansing and sterilization. Ensure equipment (30G or 32G fine needles) is ready for direct injection or device application such as MTS or Hydra Derma stamps.
2
Treatment Areas
Suitable for entire face, neck, décolleté, and back of hands. Adjust injection depth and method based on specific treatment area and individual skin condition requirements.
3
Application Method
Administer via direct syringe injection into dermal or epidermal layer. Can also be used with microneedling devices for enhanced absorption and comfort.
4
Treatment Schedule
Recommended series of 3-4 treatments spaced 2-4 weeks apart. Maintenance sessions every 6-9 months for sustained results and continued skin improvement.
