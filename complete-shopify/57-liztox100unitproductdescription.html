Professional Usage Protocol

1
Preparation & Reconstitution
Reconstitute the 100-unit vial with 2.5 mL of sterile, preservative-free 0.9% sodium chloride to achieve a concentration of 4 Units/0.1 mL. Gently swirl to dissolve - do not shake vigorously.
2
Dosage Determination
Calculate precise dosage based on treatment area: Glabellar Lines (20 units in five injection points), Crow's Feet (24 units total - 12 units per eye), Masseter Muscles (varies by individual).
3
Administration
Inject using appropriate technique for target area. Use sterile injection practices and proper needle selection for optimal results.
4
Post-Treatment Care
Use reconstituted solution within 24 hours. Monitor for immediate reactions and schedule follow-up as needed.

Safety Information & Precautions

General Precautions

  • Only for use by qualified medical professionals
  • Proper patient assessment required before treatment
  • Sterile injection technique must be maintained
  • Monitor patients for adverse reactions
  • Maintain proper cold chain storage at 2-8°C

Contraindications

  • Pregnancy and breastfeeding
  • Known allergies to botulinum toxin or albumin
  • Neuromuscular disorders
  • Active infection at injection site
  • Avoid concurrent use of other botulinum toxin products
