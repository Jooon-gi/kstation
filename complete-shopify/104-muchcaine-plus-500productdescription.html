Professional Application Guide

1
Skin Preparation
Thoroughly cleanse and dry the treatment area to ensure optimal cream absorption and effectiveness.
2
Cream Application
Apply a thick, generous layer of cream to the target area. Use maximum 0.5g for small areas as recommended.
3
Occlusive Covering
For enhanced absorption, cover the cream with plastic wrap or occlusive dressing. This significantly improves penetration.
4
Wait Period
Allow 15-30 minutes for standard procedures, or 30-60 minutes for extensive treatments like tattooing.
5
Removal & Procedure
Remove the dressing and gently wipe off excess cream just before beginning the procedure.

Storage & Safety Information

Proper Storage Conditions

Store at controlled room temperature (1-30°C or 34-86°F) in an airtight container. Avoid excessive heat, cold, and direct sunlight. Do not freeze.
Important Safety Precautions
For professional external use on intact skin only. Read all precautions before use.
  • Do not use on people with hypersensitivity to lidocaine or amide anesthetics
  • Avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes
  • Do not use in large quantities over raw or blistered surfaces
  • Stop use if allergic reaction occurs or symptoms worsen
  • Keep out of reach of children
  • Not recommended for pregnant women due to high lidocaine concentration
  • May cause skin irritation or dermatitis in some individuals
  • Consult a doctor if symptoms do not improve within 7 days
