Q. How does POWER FILL differ from traditional dermal fillers?

A. Unlike hyaluronic acid fillers, POWER FILL is a biostimulator that works by stimulating your body's natural collagen production. While HA fillers provide immediate volume that gradually diminishes, POWER FILL creates lasting results through neocollagenesis that can persist for up to 5 years.

Q. What areas can be treated with POWER FILL?

A. POWER FILL is commonly used for buttock augmentation, male genital enhancement, facial contouring (cheeks, cheekbones), and hand rejuvenation. It's specifically designed for body contouring and areas requiring significant volume restoration.

Q. How long before results are visible?

A. Initial volume is visible immediately after treatment, but optimal results develop gradually over 2-4 weeks as new collagen formation occurs. The full effect continues to improve for several months.

Q. Is a second treatment session necessary?

A. While many patients achieve desired results with one session, a second treatment can be performed approximately one month after the initial treatment for enhanced results if needed.

Q. What makes the porous structure important?

A. The porous microparticle structure allows for cell migration and tissue integration, which is crucial for natural collagen formation and long-lasting results. This design promotes better biocompatibility and integration with existing tissue.
