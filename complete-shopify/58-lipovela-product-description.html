Application Protocol & Usage Guidelines

Conduct thorough evaluation of treatment areas, mark injection sites, and prepare skin with appropriate antiseptic protocols. Ensure patient suitability and discuss expected outcomes.
2
Solution Preparation & Equipment Setup
Prepare LIPOVELA solution at room temperature, select appropriate needle gauge, and ensure all injection equipment is sterile and ready for application.
3
Subcutaneous Injection Application
Inject 0.05-0.2cc per point into subcutaneous fat layer with injection points spaced approximately 1cm apart. Use appropriate volume per treatment area as specified in protocol guidelines.

Monitor immediate treatment response, provide post-care instructions, and schedule appropriate follow-up appointments based on individual response and treatment protocol.

Frequently Asked Questions

How does LIPOVELA work to eliminate fat cells? ▼
LIPOVELA uses a dual-action mechanism combining sodium deoxycholate and phosphatidylcholine. Sodium deoxycholate acts as a detergent to destroy fat cell membranes, while PPC penetrates and dissolves the fat cells. L-Carnitine and B vitamins then accelerate the metabolic breakdown and elimination of released fatty acids through the body's natural processes.
What is the difference between LIPOVELA and LIPOVELA-V? ▼
LIPOVELA is designed for body areas like abdomen, thighs, and arms with higher volume capabilities (up to 10ml per site). LIPOVELA-V is specifically formulated for facial contouring with growth factors (EGF, FGF, IGF) and peptides for additional skin tightening benefits, using smaller volumes (2-4ml per area) suitable for delicate facial tissues.
How many treatment sessions are typically required? ▼
Body treatments typically require 2-3 sessions spaced 15 days apart for optimal results. Facial treatments with LIPOVELA-V generally need 2-4 sessions with 4-6 week intervals. Individual results may vary based on treatment area, fat volume, and patient response. Full effects are usually visible 6-8 weeks after the final session.
What post-treatment care is recommended? ▼
Patients should drink plenty of water to support the elimination process, avoid massaging treated areas for 24-48 hours, and refrain from hot tubs, saunas, and strenuous exercise for several days. Mild swelling and tenderness are normal and typically resolve within a few days. Follow all specific post-care instructions provided by your practitioner.
Are there any contraindications or restrictions? ▼
LIPOVELA should not be used in patients with soybean allergies, pregnant or breastfeeding women, or in areas below the knees where nerve density is high. Patients with certain medical conditions or taking specific medications should consult with their healthcare provider. Professional assessment is essential before treatment initiation.

Storage & Handling Requirements

LIPOVELA products require specific storage conditions to maintain potency and safety. Unopened vials should be stored at room temperature below 30°C in a dry, dark environment. Once a vial is punctured, it must be refrigerated at 5-10°C and used within a maximum of 7 days to ensure sterility and effectiveness.
