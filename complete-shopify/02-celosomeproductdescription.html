Application Guide

Product Selection Guide

Which Product is Right for Me?

Choose a product with different viscosity and injection layers depending on the treatment area and soft tissue volume. For natural improvement of fine lines, choose Soft. For structural volume enhancement, choose Implant. Mid and Strong variants provide intermediate solutions for moderate concerns.
  • Fine Lines & Delicate Areas: Celosome Soft - Superficial dermis injection for subtle corrections
  • Moderate Wrinkles & Volumizing: Celosome Mid - Middle to deep dermis for balanced enhancement
  • Deep Wrinkles & Contouring: Celosome Strong - Deep dermis for pronounced corrections
  • Structural Enhancement: Celosome Implant - Subcutaneous/supraperiosteal for major contouring
