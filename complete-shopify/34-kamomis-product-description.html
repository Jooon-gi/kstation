Q. What is the recommended dilution for Kamomis 100U?

A. Standard dilution varies based on patient needs and treatment area. Typically 2.5ml of 0.9% sterile saline to achieve 4U/0.1ml concentration, or 1.25ml for 8U/0.1ml concentration.

Q. How long do the effects of Kamomis 100U last?

A. Effects generally last for approximately 3 to 4 months. Onset of action is typically observed within 2-3 days, with maximum effect reached at 1-2 weeks.

Q. What is the recommended dose for glabellar lines?

A. For glabellar lines, the recommended total dose is 20U, administered as five 4U injections into the corrugator and procerus muscles.

Q. How should Kamomis 100U be stored?
