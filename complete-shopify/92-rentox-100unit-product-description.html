‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How do I choose between RENTOX 100U and 200U?

A. RENTOX 100U is ideal for facial cosmetic procedures including glabellar lines, forehead wrinkles, and crow's feet. Choose RENTOX 200U for larger treatment areas or combined facial and body applications such as trapezius, calves, or masseter muscle treatments.

Q. What makes RENTOX different from other botulinum toxin products?

A. RENTOX features high purity formulation free from proteins, albumin, and gelatin, which minimizes diffusion and reduces the risk of antibody resistance development. This results in more targeted and longer-lasting effects.

Q. How long does RENTOX treatment last?

A. RENTOX effects typically last 6 to 8 months on average. Initial effects become noticeable around day 3, with maximum cosmetic benefit achieved within 1-2 weeks of treatment.

Q. What is the proper storage method for RENTOX?

A. Store RENTOX vials in refrigerated conditions at 2-8¬∞C before reconstitution. Once reconstituted with sterile saline, use immediately and discard any unused solution within 24 hours.

Q. Can RENTOX be used for therapeutic applications?

A. Yes, RENTOX is effective for therapeutic use including treatment of blepharospasm and hemifacial spasm conditions, providing relief from involuntary muscle contractions and spasms.

Q. What precautions should be taken before RENTOX treatment?

A. Patients should avoid saunas, hot baths, and blood-thinning medications before treatment. Ensure no active infection at injection sites and inform practitioners of any neuromuscular disorders or medication allergies.
