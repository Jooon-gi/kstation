APPLICATION PROTOCOL & USAGE GUIDE
TREATMENT AREAS & DOSAGE
Slim Point Face: Jawline, double chin, cheeks, fleshy face areas - 0.2cc to 0.5cc per injection point
Slim Point Body: Abdomen, love handles, thighs, hips, arms, back - 0.2cc to 0.5cc per injection point
INJECTION TECHNIQUE
Direct subcutaneous injection into the fatty tissue layer using fine-gauge needle. Grid pattern injection with points spaced at least 1cm apart. For facial areas, use thin-gauge needle with precision technique.
TREATMENT SCHEDULE
Minimum 2 weeks interval between sessions for the same treatment area. Multiple sessions (2-6 treatments) may be required for optimal results depending on the area size and fat density.
POST-TREATMENT CARE
After injection, gentle manual massage or electronic massaging device can enhance solution distribution. Stay hydrated to support fat elimination process.

PRECAUTIONS & SAFETY INFORMATION
Important safety guidelines for proper product use and optimal treatment outcomes
  • Professional assessment of injection area recommended before treatment
  • Minimum 2 weeks interval required between sessions for same area
  • Avoid vigorous exercise for 24-48 hours post-treatment
  • Avoid heat exposure (saunas, hot baths) for 24-48 hours
  • Stay well-hydrated to support fat elimination process
  • Monitor injection sites for any unusual reactions
  • Store in cool, dry place away from direct sunlight
  • Use sterile injection techniques and equipment
STORAGE CONDITIONS
