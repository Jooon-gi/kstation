STEP-BY-STEP USAGE PROTOCOL
1
PREPARATION & RECONSTITUTION
Reconstitute the freeze-dried powder with sterile distilled water. Optional: mix with 1-2 mL of 2% Lidocaine for enhanced patient comfort during treatment.
2
NEEDLE PREPARATION
Use 18G needle to withdraw the reconstituted solution, then switch to 26G needle for precise injection into the targeted treatment area.
3
INJECTION TECHNIQUE
Inject into dermal layer or subcutaneous layer depending on treatment area. The spherical PLA particles ensure smooth injection with low pressure requirements.
4
TREATMENT AREAS
Apply to entire facial area excluding glabella and eye region. Suitable for deep forehead wrinkles, nasolabial folds, temporal and cheekbone augmentation.
5
POST-TREATMENT CARE
Monitor patient response and schedule follow-up treatments as needed. Treatment cycle consists of 1-3 sessions for optimal results.
TREATMENT FREQUENCY & TIMING
Recommended treatment cycle of 1-3 treatments. Single treatment session provides immediate volume for 3-9 months, with secondary efficacy from collagen regeneration lasting 6-24 months.
APPLICATION PROTOCOLS
INJECTION LAYER SPECIFICATIONS
Target dermal layer or subcutaneous layer based on treatment objectives. Deep dermis injection for wrinkle correction, subcutaneous for volume restoration.
NEEDLE GAUGE PROTOCOL
