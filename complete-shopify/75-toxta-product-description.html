Storage & Handling
Storage Requirements — Keep sealed and store in refrigerator between 2-8°C (36-46°F) at all times.
After Reconstitution — Use immediately after preparation. If not used immediately, store in refrigerator (2-8°C) and discard after 24 hours.

Post-Treatment Precautions

  • Do not press or rub the injection area to prevent toxin spreading
  • Avoid smoking, drinking, sauna, and strenuous exercise for at least two weeks
  • After masseter muscle treatment, avoid chewing hard foods
  • Follow all post-care instructions provided by your practitioner
