Active Ingredients & Formulation

Ascorbic Acid (Vitamin C)
10g/20mL Ascorbic acid is a powerful antioxidant that protects cells from damage by free radicals. It serves as a cofactor in collagen synthesis, crucial for skin elasticity and repair. Additionally, it reduces melanin production by inhibiting tyrosinase enzyme, leading to brighter skin tone.

Storage & Handling Requirements

Store in light-resistant, hermetic container between 2°C and 8°C. The solution is preservative-free. Once the vial is punctured, any unused portion must be discarded within 4 hours to prevent microbial contamination.

Safety Information & Precautions

Important Safety Guidelines
This product must be diluted before intravenous administration and requires prescription use in clinical settings only.
  • Do not administer undiluted
  • High doses may cause gastrointestinal discomfort
  • May affect urine acidity levels
  • Professional medical supervision required

Contraindications

Use with caution in patients with renal impairment, history of kidney stones, or certain genetic disorders. Should not be administered to patients with hyperoxaluria. Consult medical professional before use if pregnant, breastfeeding, or taking other medications.
