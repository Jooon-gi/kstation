SHAPE Technology

Advanced Cross-linking Process

Proprietary SHAPE (Stabilized Hyaluronic Acid and Purification Enhancement) technology involves a two-step cross-linking process for enhanced safety and longevity.

High Purity Standards

The cross-linked HA gel is highly purified to minimize uncross-linked BDDE (less than 0.4 ppm), ensuring safety and a longer-lasting effect.

Safety Information & Storage Guidelines

Important Precautions - Do not freeze or heat the product. Avoid direct sunlight. Do not put any force or pressure on the product. The product is intended for use by trained medical professionals only.
