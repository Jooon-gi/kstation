Storage & Safety Information

Storage Requirements

  • Store at 2°C ~ 25°C in a cool, dry environment
  • Protect from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures
  • Use reconstituted product within 72 hours
  • Keep vial tightly sealed when not in use
Important Precautions — Avoid use in patients with history of keloid formation or hypertrophic scarring. Common injection-related reactions may include swelling, pain, or hemorrhage.
Post-Treatment Care — Patients should avoid saunas, swimming pools, and vigorous exercise for at least one week. Proper massage technique should be performed as instructed.

Frequently Asked Questions
How does MENNUS PLUS differ from traditional fillers?
Unlike temporary fillers, MENNUS PLUS is a biostimulator that works by encouraging the body to produce its own collagen, leading to natural and gradual improvement that lasts 18-24 months.
What is the reconstitution process?
Mix the 360mg PLA powder with 8-10ml of sterile distilled water and 1-2ml of 2% Lidocaine. The freeze-drying technology allows for faster dilution and smoother injection.
How many treatment sessions are typically needed?
Generally 1-3 sessions are recommended depending on individual patient needs and desired outcomes. The treatment plan should be customized for each patient.
What are the expected results timeline?
Initial volume effect from the CMC gel component lasts approximately 3-9 months, while the secondary effect from collagen regeneration provides lasting results for 18-24 months.
