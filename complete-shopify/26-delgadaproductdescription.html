Key Ingredients & Mechanism of Action

Deoxycholic Acid
Primary active ingredient that induces the destruction of fat cells (lipolysis). This naturally occurring bile acid breaks down fat cell membranes, allowing the contents to be safely metabolized and eliminated through the body's lymphatic system.
Carnitine
Supporting compound that enhances fat metabolism and cellular energy production. Works synergistically with deoxycholic acid to optimize the fat breakdown process and promote efficient elimination of metabolized fat cells.

Safety Information & Precautions

Storage & Handling Guidelines
Store at room temperature (25°C) away from direct sunlight. Once opened, vials should be used immediately and not stored for later use. Do not exceed the maximum recommended dosage per session.
Professional Use Only
This product is intended for professional use by qualified practitioners only. Proper injection technique and safety protocols must be followed. Ensure sterile conditions and appropriate post-treatment care.
Treatment Considerations
The mechanism specifically targets fat cells without impacting surrounding protein-rich tissues like muscle and skin. For optimal results, a complete course of treatment over several sessions is recommended.
