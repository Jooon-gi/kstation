‚ö†Ô∏è Important Safety Information

OLIDIA should only be administered by qualified healthcare professionals trained in injection techniques. Proper patient assessment and injection protocols are essential for safe and effective treatment.

  • Avoid injection into blood vessels to prevent serious complications
  • Not recommended for injection into thin skin areas (glabella, periorbital)
  • Do not inject into the vermilion border of lips
  • Use immediately after reconstitution or within 72 hours if refrigerated
Contraindications & Restrictions
  • Avoid areas with active skin infections or inflammation
  • Not suitable for patients with autoimmune conditions affecting connective tissue
  • Pregnancy and breastfeeding contraindicated
  • Areas with high vascularity require special consideration
Post-Treatment Care Instructions
  • Perform gentle massage 5 minutes, 5 times daily for 5 days
  • Avoid alcohol consumption and smoking during healing period
  • Protect treated areas from excessive sun exposure
  • Contact healthcare provider if unusual swelling or reactions occur

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q. How does OLIDIA differ from traditional dermal fillers?

A. Unlike traditional fillers that provide immediate volume, OLIDIA stimulates your body's natural collagen production for gradual, long-lasting results that can extend beyond 2 years. The results appear more natural as they develop over weeks.

Q. When will I see results after OLIDIA treatment?

A. Results appear gradually over 4-6 weeks as new collagen forms. Peak effects are typically visible at 3-6 months post-treatment, with continued improvement possible for several months thereafter.

Q. How many treatment sessions are required?

A. A typical treatment cycle consists of 3 sessions spaced 1 month apart. This protocol optimizes collagen stimulation and ensures comprehensive volume restoration for long-lasting results.

Q. Is OLIDIA suitable for all skin types?

A. OLIDIA's synthetic PLLA formulation is biocompatible and suitable for most skin types. Since it contains no animal-derived ingredients, pre-treatment allergy testing is not required.

Q. What areas can be treated with OLIDIA?

A. OLIDIA is effective for facial wrinkles, nasolabial folds, marionette lines, and cheek volume restoration. It can also be used for neck, hand, and body contouring applications.

Q. Are there any side effects or downtime?

A. Common side effects include temporary swelling, bruising, or tenderness at injection sites. These typically resolve within a few days. Following post-treatment massage protocols helps optimize results.
