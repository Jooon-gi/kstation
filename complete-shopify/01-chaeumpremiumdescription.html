Safety Information & Precautions

Important Safety Guidelines
For professional use only. This product should only be administered by qualified healthcare professionals with appropriate training in dermal filler injection techniques. Store at room temperature and use immediately upon opening. Single-use product - do not reuse or resterilize.
Post-Treatment Precautions
Patients should avoid touching or massaging the treated area for 24 hours. Refrain from strenuous physical activity, alcohol consumption, and exposure to extreme heat or cold for 48 hours. Mild redness, swelling, or bruising at injection site is normal and typically resolves within a few days.
Contraindications
Do not use if pregnant or breastfeeding. Not recommended for patients with known hypersensitivity to hyaluronic acid or lidocaine. Avoid in areas with active skin infection or inflammation. Consult medical professional for complete list of contraindications and drug interactions.

Storage & Handling Instructions

Storage Conditions
Store at room temperature in original packaging. Protect from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures. Do not freeze or expose to temperatures above 25°C (77°F). Keep in dry environment away from moisture.
Handling Guidelines
Handle syringes with care to prevent damage. Use sterile technique when preparing for injection. Check product integrity before use - do not use if packaging is damaged or product appears compromised.
Shelf Life Information
Product maintains stability and effectiveness when stored under recommended conditions. Check expiration date before use. Single-use product - discard after opening and do not attempt to preserve for future use.
