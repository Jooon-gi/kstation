Safety Information & Precautions

Important Safety Guidelines

This product is intended for use by trained medical professionals only. Proper injection technique and sterile conditions are essential for safe and effective treatment.
  • Temporary swelling, redness, or bruising may occur at injection sites
  • Maintain minimum 3-day intervals between treatments
  • Avoid treatment during pregnancy or breastfeeding
  • Conduct patch test for sensitivity before full treatment

Contraindications

Do not use in patients with active infections, autoimmune disorders, bleeding disorders, or known allergies to any component. Avoid treatment in areas with existing skin conditions or recent cosmetic procedures.

Post-Treatment Care

Apply cold compress if needed, avoid excessive heat or sun exposure for 24-48 hours. Gentle massage may help distribute product and reduce swelling. Contact healthcare provider if severe or persistent reactions occur.
