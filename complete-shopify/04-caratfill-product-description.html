Treatment Protocol

1

Pre-Treatment Assessment

Evaluate patient's medical history, skin condition, and treatment goals. Determine appropriate CARATFILL variant based on treatment area and desired depth of injection.

2

Preparation & Anesthesia

Cleanse treatment area and apply topical anesthetic if needed. Allow CARATFILL to reach room temperature before injection. Select appropriate needle gauge based on product variant.

3

Injection Technique

Inject using appropriate technique for selected layer (upper dermis, mid-deep dermis, or subcutis). Use slow, controlled injection with gentle massage for optimal distribution.

4

Post-Treatment Care

Apply cold compress if needed. Provide aftercare instructions including avoiding alcohol, saunas, and excessive facial manipulation for several days post-treatment.
