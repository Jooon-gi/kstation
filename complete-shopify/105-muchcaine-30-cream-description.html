Application Guide

1
Clean the Application Area
Ensure the skin is clean and dry before application. Remove any oils, lotions, or debris from the treatment area.
2
Apply Thick Layer
Spread a thick layer of cream over the desired area. Maximum recommended dose for adults is 0.5g. Avoid using on large areas or broken skin.
3
Cover if Needed
Optional: Cover with occlusive dressing or plastic wrap to enhance absorption, though not always required.
4
Wait for Effect
Allow 15-30 minutes for most procedures. For more intensive treatments like hair removal or tattoos, wait up to 1 hour.
5
Remove Before Procedure
Gently wipe off the cream just before beginning the procedure. The numbing effect will remain active.

Important Safety Information
Do not use if you have a known sensitivity to lidocaine or other amide preparations. Do not apply to mucous membranes, open wounds, or in or near the eyes. Stop use and consult a doctor if an allergic reaction occurs. Consult a medical professional before use, especially if you have liver or kidney conditions, or are pregnant.

Storage & Handling
