üí° Therapeutic Benefits & Effects

Cosmetic Applications

Temporary improvement of moderate to severe facial wrinkles caused by muscle activity, including glabellar lines, crow's feet, and forehead lines. Results typically appear within 2-5 days with full effects visible in 10-14 days.

Wrinkle Reduction Facial Rejuvenation Non-Surgical Treatment

Therapeutic Applications

Effective treatment for benign essential blepharospasm, equinus foot deformity in pediatric cerebral palsy patients, and upper limb spasticity following stroke.

  • Reduces involuntary muscle contractions and spasticity
  • Improves functional mobility and quality of life
  • Provides long-lasting relief lasting 3-5 months
  • Minimally invasive alternative to surgical intervention

Duration and Onset

Initial effects typically visible within 2-5 days post-injection, with peak results achieved in 10-14 days. Treatment effects generally last 3-4 months for cosmetic applications and 3-5 months for therapeutic uses.

üß™ Usage Protocols & Administration

1
Preparation & Reconstitution
Reconstitute with 2.5-4 mL of 0.9% preservative-free saline. Gently swirl to dissolve powder without shaking to prevent denaturation of the active botulinum toxin.
2
Dosage Calculation
Calculate appropriate dosage based on treatment area: 20 units for glabellar lines (5 injection sites), individualized dosing for therapeutic applications based on muscle size and severity.
3
Administration Technique
Perform intramuscular injections using sterile technique. For deep muscles, electromyographic guidance may be recommended to ensure accurate placement and optimal results.
4
Post-Treatment Care
Monitor for initial effects within 2-5 days. Full results typically achieved in 10-14 days. Schedule follow-up appointments and plan next treatment session after 3-4 months.

Important Safety Information
MEDITOXIN should only be administered by qualified medical professionals. Proper storage, handling, and injection techniques are essential for safe and effective treatment.
  • Store unopened vials at 2-8¬∞C or below -5¬∞C, protected from light
  • Use reconstituted solution within 24 hours and store refrigerated
  • Do not shake vial during reconstitution to prevent toxin denaturation
  • Contraindicated in patients with neuromuscular disorders or botulinum toxin allergies
  • Maximum cumulative dose should not exceed 200 units within 30 days for certain indications
  • Electromyographic guidance recommended for deep muscle injections
Always consult product labeling and seek professional medical guidance before administration. Report any adverse reactions to appropriate medical authorities.
