Usage Protocol & Administration

1
Reconstitution Preparation
Use sterile, preservative-free 0.9% Sodium Chloride Injection. Inject diluent slowly against vial wall to prevent foaming.
2
Gentle Mixing
Gently swirl the vial to ensure complete dissolution. Do not shake vigorously to maintain protein integrity.
3
Dose Calculation
Calculate appropriate dosage based on treatment area and patient needs. Use within 24 hours of reconstitution.
4
Injection Technique
Use fine needles (27-30G) for precise intramuscular or intradermal injection depending on indication.

Safety Information & Precautions

Important Safety Information

This product should only be administered by qualified medical professionals with appropriate training in botulinum toxin injection techniques.
  • Not for use in pregnant or nursing women
  • Contraindicated in patients with myasthenia gravis
  • Avoid use with aminoglycoside antibiotics
  • Monitor for signs of botulism or distant spread

Contraindications

Hypersensitivity to botulinum toxin or any component, infection at injection site, myasthenia gravis, Lambert-Eaton syndrome, or other neuromuscular disorders.

Emergency Response

In case of adverse reactions, seek immediate medical attention. Have emergency equipment available and monitor patients for signs of systemic botulinum toxin effects.
