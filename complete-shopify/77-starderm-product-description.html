CONTRAINDICATIONS & PRECAUTIONS
Important contraindications and precautions for safe product administration and optimal patient outcomes.
  • Not recommended for patients with known hypersensitivity to hyaluronic acid or lidocaine
  • Avoid injection in areas with active inflammation or infection
  • Exercise caution in patients with bleeding disorders or taking anticoagulants
  • Not suitable for pregnant or breastfeeding patients
  • Avoid injection near permanent implants or fillers
  • Consider patient medical history and current medications
  • Perform thorough consultation and consent process
  • Monitor for delayed hypersensitivity reactions
