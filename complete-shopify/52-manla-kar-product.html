Usage Instructions & Application Methods

Professional Application Protocol

  1. Preparation Phase — Ensure sterile conditions and prepare mixing solution according to chosen application method
  2. Product Mixing — Mix MANLA KAR with appropriate solution based on application technique (see methods below)
  3. Treatment Application — Apply using selected injection method with proper depth and technique
  4. Post-Treatment Care — Follow standard post-injection care protocols for optimal results

Direct Injection Method

  • Mix product with 1mL saline in 1:1 ratio
  • Use for targeted treatment of fine wrinkles and dark circles
  • Suitable for precise application in delicate areas

Water Light Needle Method

  • Mix with 2-3mL saline and water light needle solution in 1:1:1 ratio
  • Use 4mm microneedle for optimal penetration
  • Ideal for broader facial treatment areas

MTS (Microneedle Therapy System)

  • Can be combined with MTS for enhanced absorption
  • Suitable for comprehensive facial regeneration
  • Often used in combination with other treatments
