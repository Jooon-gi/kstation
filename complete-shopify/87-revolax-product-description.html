Safety Information & Precautions

Professional Use Only — This product is intended for use by qualified medical professionals only.

Important Contraindications

  • Do not inject into the eye area or blood vessels
  • Avoid use in pregnant or nursing women
  • Not recommended for individuals under 18
  • Do not use in areas with active inflammation or infection
  • Single use only - do not re-sterilize or reuse

Post-Treatment Care

  1. Avoid prolonged sun exposure for 2 weeks
  2. Avoid extreme temperatures (hot/cold)
  3. Avoid UV light exposure
  4. Follow professional aftercare instructions
