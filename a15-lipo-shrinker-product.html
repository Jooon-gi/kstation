Product Overview

Lipo Shrinker is a professional-grade lipolytic solution that combines phosphatidylcholine (PPC) and deoxycholate with circulation-promoting agents to effectively target and eliminate stubborn localized fat deposits. This advanced formulation works through a dual-action mechanism: deoxycholate emulsifies fat and destroys fat cell membranes, while PPC breaks down and dissolves the remaining lipids. The addition of L-Carnitine and supporting ingredients promotes lymphatic drainage, enabling natural excretion of dissolved fat.

Key Ingredients & Formulation

Active Components
Phosphatidylcholine (PPC)

A phospholipid that effectively breaks down and dissolves fat cells, working synergistically with deoxycholate to target adipose tissue and promote fat metabolism.

Deoxycholate

A bile acid that emulsifies fat and disrupts fat cell membranes, enabling the breakdown and elimination of localized fat deposits.

L-Carnitine

An amino acid derivative that supports fat metabolism and enhances lymphatic drainage, facilitating the body's natural process of eliminating dissolved fat.

Circulation-Promoting Extracts

Supporting botanical extracts that enhance microcirculation and promote efficient drainage of metabolized fat through the lymphatic system.

Synergistic Mechanism of Action

The formulation works through a coordinated three-phase process: First, deoxycholate emulsifies and disrupts fat cell membranes. Second, PPC penetrates and dissolves the released lipids. Finally, L-Carnitine and circulation enhancers promote lymphatic drainage, allowing the body to naturally excrete the broken-down fat. This comprehensive approach ensures effective fat reduction with minimized downtime.

Treatment Protocol & Application

Professional Administration Guidelines

Treatment Areas

Lipo Shrinker is suitable for facial contouring (jawline, double chin, cheeks) and body applications (abdomen, flanks/love handles, inner and outer thighs, arms). The solution is injected into the subcutaneous fat layer using sterile technique.

Recommended Treatment Cycle

Typically, 3-6 sessions are recommended, performed at intervals of 1-2 weeks. Individual results may vary based on treatment area, fat volume, and patient response. A comprehensive assessment should determine the optimal number of sessions.

1
Area Preparation
Cleanse and disinfect the treatment area thoroughly. Mark injection points using appropriate mapping technique for even distribution.
2
Product Preparation
Lipo Shrinker is ready-to-use and requires no dilution. Draw the appropriate volume into a sterile syringe with 27G or 30G needle.
3
Injection Technique
Inject into the subcutaneous fat layer using appropriate depth and volume per point. Facial: 0.1-0.2ml per point in fine grid pattern. Body: larger volumes distributed evenly across treatment zone.
4
Post-Treatment Care
Apply gentle massage to distribute product evenly. Advise patient on expected temporary side effects including swelling, bruising, and tenderness.
5
Follow-Up Protocol
Schedule next session in 1-2 weeks. Encourage adequate water intake and gentle lymphatic massage to optimize drainage and results.

Safety Information & Precautions

Important Storage Requirements
Store in a cool, dark place at temperatures between 2-30¬∞C. Do not freeze. Keep away from direct sunlight. Proper storage ensures product stability and effectiveness.
Treatment Interval Requirements
A minimum interval of 1-2 weeks between sessions is crucial to allow the body sufficient time to process and eliminate dissolved fat. Do not exceed recommended treatment frequency.
Contraindications
Do not use on pregnant or lactating women, individuals with severe liver or kidney disease, diabetes, or known allergies to ingredients (especially soy or deoxycholate). A thorough medical history assessment is required before treatment.
Expected Post-Treatment Effects
Mild swelling, bruising, and tenderness at injection sites are common and temporary. These effects typically resolve within 3-7 days. Avoid excessive heat exposure (saunas, intense exercise) for 24-48 hours post-procedure.

Post-Treatment Care Guidelines

Immediate Care (24-48 hours)

Avoid heat exposure, intense physical activity, and alcohol consumption. Apply cold compresses if needed to reduce swelling. Keep the treated area clean and dry.

Recovery Period (1-2 weeks)

Increase water intake to support lymphatic drainage. Gentle massage can help distribute product and promote fat elimination. Avoid anti-inflammatory medications unless approved by practitioner.
