## 0. Product Name: NEURAMIS (Lidocaine / Light Lidocaine / Deep Lidocaine / Deep Non-Lido / Volume Lidocaine)
- The NEURAMIS series is a line of hyaluronic acid dermal fillers manufactured by Medytox Inc. It includes several versions tailored for different aesthetic needs.
- The product is typically packaged as a pre-filled syringe containing 1ml of the product.

## 1. Key Ingredients & Concentration
- The primary active ingredient is Hyaluronic Acid (HA).
- Hyaluronic Acid Concentration: 20 mg/ml
- Lidocaine Concentration: 0.3% (for versions with Lidocaine)

## 2. Efficacy Benefits
- Reduction of superficial to deep wrinkles and folds.
- Facial volume restoration and enhancement.
- Facial contouring, including cheeks and chin.
- Skin hydration and texture improvement.

## 3. Protocol (Instructions)
- Injection Layer by Treatment Area: Varies by product version, from the upper dermis for fine lines to deep subcutaneous layers for volume restoration.
- Recommended Needle Gauge: Varies by product version, commonly 30G or 27G.
- Treatment Cycle: The aesthetic effect typically lasts for 6 to 9 months, depending on the specific product and treatment area.

---

## 4. Key Ingredients and Technical Features
- Cross-linking Method: The products are manufactured using the proprietary SHAPE (Stabilized Hyaluronic Acid and Purification Enhancement) technology, which involves a two-step cross-linking process.
- Gel Structure: The cross-linked HA gel is highly purified to minimize uncross-linked BDDE (less than 0.4 ppm), ensuring safety and a longer-lasting effect.
- Formulation Type: Each product comes in a sterile 1 ml pre-filled syringe.

## Which product is right for me?
Choose a product with different viscosity and injection layers depending on the treatment area and soft tissue volume. For natural enhancement of fine lines, the Light version is ideal. For a more pronounced volume effect, the Volume version is recommended. For general wrinkles and folds, the versatile Lidocaine and Deep Lidocaine options provide targeted solutions.
