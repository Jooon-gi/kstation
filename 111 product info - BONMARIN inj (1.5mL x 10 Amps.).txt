## 6. Precautions and Storage
- **Precautions**: Do not use if you have hypercalcemia, hypercalciuria, calcium oxalate stones, or sarcoidosis. Overdose can lead to adverse effects such as headache, muscle pain, nausea, and vomiting. Use under the guidance of a medical professional.

- **Shelf life after opening**: Must be used immediately after the ampoule is opened.
